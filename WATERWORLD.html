WaterWorld

WaterWorld Iloilo is the biggest and first-ever waterpark in the region located within the Eon Centennial Resort Hotel and Waterpark complex at Datu Sumakwel St., Alta Tierra Village in Jaro, Iloilo City. It is an amusement and leisure park with rides, slides, pools, food outlets, and other fun amenities which all ages will surely enjoy. The waterpark has around 20 colorful and extreme rides and waterslides, some are as tall as 4-story building. An enormous water playhouse FuntaSea with more rides and slides is located indoors covered with roofing full of solar panels, which power the whole facility.

Waterworld also features the relaxing Lazy River that is perfect for unwinding the day away and that thrilling Mat Racers that will surely pump your adrenaline. WaterWorld offers an all-day unlimited use of all slides and attractions for the promo rate of only P350 (with Php150 worth of food coupons redeemable at any WaterWorld food outlet). The rate applies to all who wish to enter the waterpark, except for kids 3 feet and below who are FREE. Wearing proper swimwear is strictly imposed when engaging in all water rides and pool activities. Board Shorts, Swimsuits, Bikinis, Rashguards, Full body Swimsuits, Swimming Diapers, and Goggles are allowed. Not allowed are Regular Diapers, Undergarments, Jeans, Jackets, Halter Dress, Denim Shorts, Long Skirts, Garments with Buttons and Zippers, Boxer Shorts, and Jewelry.

'You have not been to Iloilo if you have not been to Waterworld', these are the words of Mr. Felix Tiu, the person behind the two (2) hectare property known as Waterworld Iloilo, which will officially open to the public today, December 16, 2016. Waterworld Iloilo is the first waterpark in the Visayas and the first in the world to be powered by solar energy. Waterworld Iloilo offers extreme water rides, 17 slides for kids and kids at heart, a 222 meter long lazy river, a water play area for toddlers, an open and covered waterpark, all manned by 30 certified lifeguards. 'Safety is our top priority' Tiu adds. The said waterpark also offers automated lockers, aircon suites, themed rooms, and lounge chairs. Waterworld Iloilo aims to help Iloilo City increase tourism arrivals by creating a destination place in the City of Love. Aside from that, it aims to increase job opportunities and improve lives, help the city government's tax revenue by attracting people from other places, improve branding and profile of Iloilo City as a tourist destination for both local and international visitors, and become a travel mart for tourists to visit before they go home. Alongside Waterworld Iloilo is EON Centennial Resort Hotel which will be marking its 10th anniversary this year.

How to get there

To reach WaterWorld, you can follow these general directions:

Waterworld is located at Datu Sumakwel St., Alta Tierra Village, Iloilo City. It's very easy to locate since it inside the subdivision. Just take taxi and tell the driver to drop you off at WaterWorld Iloilo. If you want to commute, you may take Jaro-Liko or Leganes jeepneys and alight at the entrance of Alta Tierra Subdivision. You may take a pedicab or tricycle going to the water park.

It's recommended to verify the specific routes and transportation options based on your starting point and current travel conditions. Additionally, always exercise caution and follow local traffic regulations while traveling.
