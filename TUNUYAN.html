Tinuy-an Falls

Tinuy-an Falls stands as a majestic testament to nature's splendor, nestled amidst the verdant forests of Bislig City, Surigao del Sur, in the Philippines. Often hailed as the "Niagara Falls of the Philippines," this breathtaking cascade captivates the senses with its sheer grandeur and pristine beauty.

As you approach Tinuy-an Falls, the thundering roar of cascading water heralds your arrival, drawing you closer to its awe-inspiring presence. Emerging from the lush greenery, the waterfall reveals itself in all its majestic glory â€” a symphony of rushing water plummeting from a height of over 55 meters, spanning an impressive width of 95 meters.

The turquoise waters of Tinuy-an Falls beckon visitors to take a refreshing dip in its natural pool. Whether you're seeking a respite from the tropical heat or simply yearning for a leisurely swim, the cool, crystal-clear waters provide the perfect oasis for relaxation and rejuvenation. Tinuy-an Falls not only offers swimming as its activity, tourists can also do Bamboo Rafting, Picnicking and Nature Appreciation, Photography and Sightseeing, and some Exploration while Hiking.

How to get there

To reach Tinuy-an Falls, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. Fly from Manila to Butuan City's Bancasi Airport (BXU) or Take a bus from Manila to Butuan City. Several bus companies offer trips to Butuan City from various terminals in Manila.

2. From Butuan City, take a bus or van to Bislig City. The trip usually takes around 5 to 6 hours. Upon reaching Bislig City, you can hire a motorcycle (habal-habal) or tricycle to take you to Tinuy-an Falls. It's approximately a 30 to 45-minute ride from Bislig City proper to the falls.

By Private Vehicle:

1. Start your journey in Manila and head southbound on the Pan-Philippine Highway (AH26) or South Luzon Expressway (SLEX). Continue on AH26 until you reach the city of Bislig in Surigao del Sur province, Mindanao. Once in Bislig, follow the signs leading to Tinuy-an Falls.

2. The journey by private vehicle typically takes around 30 to 35 hours, depending on traffic conditions, road conditions, and stops along the way.
