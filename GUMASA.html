GUMASA BEACH

Gumasa Beach, located in Glan, Sarangani, is a pristine white sand beach renowned for its crystal-clear turquoise waters and picturesque surroundings.

The beach offers a tranquil and serene atmosphere, making it an ideal destination for relaxation and beach activities.

Visitors can indulge in swimming, sunbathing, beach volleyball, or simply enjoy the breathtaking sunsets.

The powdery sand and the gentle sound of waves create a soothing ambiance, providing a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

HOW TO GET THERE?

To get to Gumasa Beach in Glan, Sarangani, there are several transportation options available:

1. By Air: The nearest airport to Gumasa Beach is General Santos International Airport (GES). From the airport, you can take a taxi or a hired car to Gumasa Beach, which is approximately a 1 to 1.5-hour drive away.

2. By Land: If you are coming from General Santos City, you can take a public utility van or jeepney bound for Glan. These vehicles can be found at the public transport terminal in General Santos City. The travel time from General Santos City to Gumasa Beach is around 1 to 1.5 hours.

If you are coming from other parts of Mindanao or the Philippines, you can take a bus bound for General Santos City. From General Santos City, you can follow the same route as mentioned above.

3. By Private Vehicle: If you have your own vehicle or are renting one, you can drive to Gumasa Beach. From General Santos City, take the General Santos City-Glan Road and follow the signs leading to Gumasa Beach. The travel time is approximately 1 to 1.5 hours, depending on traffic conditions.

Once you arrive in Glan, Gumasa Beach is easily accessible and well-known in the area. You can ask the locals for directions or follow the signs leading to the beach. It is always recommended to plan your trip in advance and check for any road closures or travel advisories before heading to Gumasa Beach.
