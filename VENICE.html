Venice grand canal

The Venice Grand Canal in the Philippines is a stunning recreation of the famous Italian waterway located within the McKinley Hill development in Taguig City, Metro Manila.The Venice Grand Canal boasts impressive architecture inspired by the romantic city of Venice, Italy.

It features picturesque bridges, colorful buildings, and authentic Italian-style facades that line the canal's banks, creating a charming and romantic ambiance.

To get to the Venice Grand Canal in Taguig City, Metro Manila, Philippines, you have several transportation options depending on your starting point:

Private Vehicle: If you're driving, you can navigate to McKinley Hill in Taguig City. The Venice Grand Canal is located within this development. You can use a GPS or navigation app to find the specific entrance and parking areas.

Public Transportation: Jeepney: You can take a jeepney that passes through McKinley Hill. Look for jeepneys with routes going to Fort Bonifacio or Guadalupe. From the nearest drop-off point, you may need to walk or take a short taxi or tricycle ride to reach the Venice Grand Canal.
