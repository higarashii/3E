To reach Belvedere, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. If you're coming from Manila or other major cities with airports, the closest major airport to Belvedere is Ozamiz City Airport. From there, you can take a bus or a taxi to reach Misamis Occidental.

2. Buses are available from major cities like Cagayan de Oro, Iligan, Zamboanga, Pagadian, and Dipolog that travel directly to Ozamiz City, which is near Misamis Occidental.

3. For those preferring a journey by water, ferries operate routes that connect to Ozamiz City from various parts such as Manila, Iligan, Tagbilaran, and Cebu.

4. Once in Ozamiz City, local buses or vans can be used to travel within Misamis Occidental and reach specific destinations like Belvedere.

By Private Vehicle:

1. To travel to Belvedere in Misamis Occidental via private vehicle, you can follow the general directions and ensure you have a reliable map or GPS service as local road conditions and signages can vary.

2. Drive west on the National Highway towards Tangub City and follow signages and local directions to reach Belvedere in Misamis Occidental.
