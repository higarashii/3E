Tuboran Eriberta Spring

Tuboran Eriberta Spring is located in Tuboran, Mahayag, Zamboanga del Sur, offers a serene escape for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike. This hidden gem boasts crystal-clear waters, lush greenery, and a tranquil ambiance.

This spring is gifted with an unceasing flow of naturally cold, clean, and pure water. It comprises four gigantic swimming pools designed for both adults and children. The pools provide a refreshing respite from the tropical heat, making Tuboran Eriberta Spring a popular destination for families, friends, and solo travelers seeking relaxation and fun.

Whether you're sliding down natural rock formations, enjoying a barbecue meal by the water, or singing your heart out at the karaoke area, Tuboran Eriberta Spring promises an unforgettable experience.

How to get there

To reach Tuboran Eriberta Spring, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. By bus or vans, from Ozamiz City, take the National Highway south toward Molave or Pagadian City.

2. Once in Molave, seek directions from local residents who are familiar with the unique cold-water spring. They can guide you to Eriberta Spring Resort.

By Private Vehicle:

1. If you're driving, ensure your vehicle has sufficient clearance. Follow the National Highway south from Ozamiz City toward Molave or Pagadian City.

2. Stop in Molave and ask locals for precise directions to the spring. The road has been paved and improved over the years, making it easier to access.

It's recommended to verify the specific routes and transportation options based on your starting point and current travel conditions. Additionally, always exercise caution and follow local traffic regulations while traveling.
