Philippine Eagle Foundation's Center for Philippine Raptors

On our 35th year of being at the forefront of saving the critically endangered Philippine eagle, we recognize our milestones that advanced our mission. And as we leap forward to many more, we are rallying the Filipino people to join us in saving eagles, protecting forests, and securing our future.

Let us celebrate how far we have come in conserving our national bird. It is time to transform awareness into commitment and action that preserve our eagle and our forests.

All birds participating in the flight demonstration are wild animals and are not to be kept as pets. The PEF has a special permit from the DENR that allows us to keep the birds at the Philippine Eagle Center.

Please be informed that the flight demonstration may be cancelled due to bad weather, unavailability of the birds or major technical difficulties.

How to get there

To reach Philippine Eagle Foundation's Center for Philippine Raptors , you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. The center is located in Malagos, Baguio District, Davao City. You can take a taxi or a private vehicle to get there.

By Private Vehicle:

1. If you're driving, you can take the Davao-Bukidnon Road and follow signs leading to Malagos. The center is well-marked, and there is ample parking available.

You can take a bus or van from Davao City bound for Calinan or Malagos. Once in Malagos, you can hire a motorcycle taxi or tricycle to take you to the Philippine Eagle Foundation's Center.
