GIBONG RIVER

Gibong River is located in Poblacion, Prosperidad, Agusan del Sur. Gibong River is just one of the ideal destinations in Agusan del Sur for river cruising. It is a wide and calm river with abundant greenery on its banks. If you are one of those nature lovers and seeks serenity, surely this destination should not be missed. This river is also a source of water irrigation to more than 6,000 hectares of rice fields in its nearby municipalities and serves as the only gateway to Binaba Falls.

Gibong River, the longest river in the Municipality of Prosperidad. Partially, the pristine beauty of Gibong River at Gibong Bridge can be seen as it traverses Daang Maharlika/National Highway in Barangay Poblacion.

Prosperidad's scenic river and potable water source which is ideal for cruising and boating activities. From the river banks, visitors can cruise along the river and be enthralled by the untouched vegetation and limestone cliffs enroute to Gibong Recreation Center, a camping and bird watching site in the woods. Adjacent it is Binaba Falls, a cascade of clear water emanating from the watershed.

HOW TO GET THERE

To reach Gibong River, you can follow these general directions:

1. From Manila you can book a flight from Butuan and then ride a bus to Davao City.

2. Upon arrival at Davao, you can take a bus, car jeepney or tricycle to reach the Gibong Falls. Most drivers are familiar with the location of the River because it can seen it travesers Daang Maharlika/ National Highway in Barangay Poblacion.
