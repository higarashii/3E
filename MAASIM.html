MAASIM BEACH

Maasim Beach, located in Maasim, Sarangani, offers a serene and less crowded beach experience.

The beach provides a peaceful ambiance and breathtaking views of the sea and surrounding landscapes. Visitors can enjoy swimming in the calm waters, sunbathing on the sandy shore, or simply take in the beauty of the natural surroundings.

The beach offers a tranquil escape from the bustling city life and provides an opportunity to unwind and connect with nature.

HOT TO GET THERE?

To get to Maasim Beach in Maasim, Sarangani, you can follow these directions:

1. By Air: The nearest airport to Maasim Beach is General Santos International Airport (GES). From the airport, you can take a taxi or a hired car to Maasim, which is approximately a 30 to 45-minute drive away.

2. By Land: If you are coming from General Santos City, you can take a public utility van or jeepney bound for Maasim. These vehicles can be found at the public transport terminal in General Santos City. The travel time from General Santos City to Maasim is around 30 to 45 minutes.

If you are coming from other parts of Mindanao or the Philippines, you can take a bus bound for General Santos City. From General Santos City, you can follow the same route as mentioned above.

3. By Private Vehicle: If you have your own vehicle or are renting one, you can drive to Maasim Beach. From General Santos City, take the General Santos City-Maasim Road and follow the signs leading to Maasim Beach. The travel time is approximately 30 to 45 minutes, depending on traffic conditions.

Once you arrive in Maasim, Maasim Beach is easily accessible. You can ask the locals for directions or follow the signs leading to the beach. It is always recommended to plan your trip in advance and check for any road closures or travel advisories before heading to Maasim Beach.

Hotel 1: Lemlunay Dive Resort

Location: Tinoto, Maasim, Sarangani

Nestled on a cliff overlooking the stunning Maasim Beach, Lemlunay Dive Resort offers comfortable accommodations with breathtaking sea views. The resort is a haven for diving enthusiasts due to its proximity to some of the best dive sites in the area.

Guests can choose from well-appointed rooms and cottages, each designed with a touch of local charm and equipped with modern amenities. The resort features a restaurant serving delicious local and international cuisine, a swimming pool for a refreshing dip, and a diving center for those looking to explore the underwater wonders.

With its prime location and warm hospitality, Lemlunay Dive Resort provides a perfect getaway for beach lovers and diving enthusiasts alike.
