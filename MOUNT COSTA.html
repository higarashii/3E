Mount Costa

Mount Costa is a unique and picturesque garden located in La Trinidad, Benguet, Philippines. It is a sprawling landscape filled with various themed gardens, sculptures, and art installations, offering visitors a delightful and immersive experience.The garden covers around five hectares of land and is divided into different sections, each with its own distinct theme and design. These sections include the French Garden, Greek Garden, Spanish Garden, Japanese Garden, and many more. Each garden showcases different plants, flowers, and architectural elements that represent the specific theme

Mount Costa is not just a garden but also an art gallery in nature. Throughout the grounds, you will find a wide array of sculptures and art installations created by local and international artists. These artworks add a touch of creativity and uniqueness to the already beautiful surroundings.Visitors can leisurely explore the gardens, take photos, and appreciate the stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. There are also designated picnic areas where visitors can relax and enjoy a meal amidst the natural beauty of the garden.
