UGIS PEAK IN ESPERANZA

The Daguma Mountain Range's Ugis Peak is located in the Municipality of Esperanza, Province of Sultan Kudarat, and is the ancestral home of the T'durays of southwest Mindanao. Its difficult and nerve-wracking knife-edge peak has made it a popular destination for Mindanao mountain climbers.

A day hike to the mount's peak will introduce you to the excellent local culture of the Teduray, the natives living at the foot of Mt. Ugis, as you pass by.
