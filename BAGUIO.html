Baguio City

Baguio City, located in the province of Benguet. Its exact address is Sto. Thomas Road, Baguio City, 2600, Philippines. Baguio City. This is a popular tourist destination in the Philippines. It is known as the "Summer Capital" of the country due to its cool climate and picturesque landscapes. The city is situated on a plateau about 1,500 meters above sea level, high in the Cordillera Central mountain range. The city is surrounded by pine trees, earning it the nickname "City of Pines." With its pleasant weather, Baguio City offers a refreshing escape from the heat and congestion of other cities in the Philippines.

Today, Baguio City thrives as a major tourist destination, attracting visitors with its scenic beauty, cultural heritage, and vibrant local markets. The city is home to various attractions, including Mines View Park, Burnham Park, Baguio Cathedral, and the famous Session Road, lined with shops and restaurants offering a diverse range of cuisines.

Baguio City, Benguet offers tourists a cool and refreshing escape from the heat of other cities in the Philippines. Visitors can explore its scenic landscapes, indulge in local delicacies, and immerse themselves in the vibrant cultural heritage of the city.

How to get there

To reach Baguio City, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. Take a bus from Manila to Baguio City. Bus companies like Victory Liner and Genesis Transport offer regular trips to Baguio from various terminals in Manila. The journey typically takes around 6-7 hours, depending on traffic and road conditions.

2. Once in Baguio, you can easily navigate the city using local jeepneys, taxis, or ride-sharing services to reach your specific destination within the city.

By Private Vehicle:

1. From Manila, take the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and drive towards Baguio City. You can choose to take Kennon Road from Rosario or Marcos Highway from Agoo. Utilize navigation apps like Waze or Google Maps for the best route based on current traffic conditions.

2. Upon reaching Baguio City, there are various parking facilities available in the city center and near popular attractions where you can park your private vehicle.

Whether you choose public transportation or a private vehicle, these directions will help you reach Baguio City efficiently in the CAR region.
