Kalilangan Cold Spring

Kalilangan Cold Spring in Lanao del Norte is a renowned tourist attraction in the northern region of Mindanao. Its popularity is due to its stunning surroundings, crystal clear water, and tranquil atmosphere.It is located at Tubod, Lanao del Norte

Kalilangan Cold Spring is known for its natural beauty and has attracted visitors for a long time. It is a great location for picnicking and swimming. The spring is situated in Barangay Kalilangan, which is a tiny village within the town of Tubod, Lanao del Norte. The spring is run by an underground stream that is believed to connect to a subterranean river and is believed to have healing properties.

Also, the spring lies surrounded by thick vegetation and is an ideal place to watch birds. Spring is a well-known place to camp and for various outdoor pursuits. There are several camping spots close to the spring and plenty of room for hammocks and tents.

How to Get there

To reach Kalilangan Cold Spring, you can follow these general directions:

By Land Transport:

1. From Cagayan de Oro City, you can take a bus or a van bound for Malaybalay City, which is the capital of Bukidnon. The journey takes approximately 2-3 hours, depending on the traffic conditions and the mode of transportation.

2. Once you reach Malaybalay City, you can hire a habal-habal (a motorcycle taxi) or a tricycle to take you to Kalilangan Cold Spring. It is located in Barangay Kalasungay, which is around 8 kilometers away from the city center. You can inform the driver that you want to go to Kalilangan Cold Spring, and they will take you there.
