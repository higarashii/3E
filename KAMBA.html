KIAMBA WHITE BEACH

Kiamba White Beach, situated in Kiamba, Sarangani, is a picturesque beach destination known for its powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters.

The beach offers a peaceful and secluded atmosphere, making it an ideal spot for relaxation and beach picnics. Visitors can enjoy swimming in the refreshing waters, sunbathing on the pristine sand, or taking leisurely walks along the shore.

The beach provides breathtaking coastal views and a serene ambiance away from the crowds.

HOT TO GET THERE?

To get to Kiamba White Beach in Kiamba, Sarangani, you can follow these directions:

1. By Air: The nearest airport to Kiamba White Beach is General Santos International Airport (GES). From the airport, you can take a taxi or a hired car to Kiamba, which is approximately a 1 to 1.5-hour drive away.

2. By Land: If you are coming from General Santos City, you can take a public utility van or jeepney bound for Kiamba. These vehicles can be found at the public transport terminal in General Santos City. The travel time from General Santos City to Kiamba is around 1 to 1.5 hours.

If you are coming from other parts of Mindanao or the Philippines, you can take a bus bound for General Santos City. From General Santos City, you can follow the same route as mentioned above.

3. By Private Vehicle: If you have your own vehicle or are renting one, you can drive to Kiamba White Beach. From General Santos City, take the General Santos City-Kiamba Road and follow the signs leading to Kiamba White Beach. The travel time is approximately 1 to 1.5 hours, depending on traffic conditions.

Once you arrive in Kiamba, Kiamba White Beach is easily accessible. You can ask the locals for directions or follow the signs leading to the beach. It is always recommended to plan your trip in advance and check for any road closures or travel advisories before heading to Kiamba White Beach.
