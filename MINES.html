Mines View Park

Mines View Park is a popular tourist spot located at Mines View Observation Deck, Baguio City, Philippines. Situated on a hilltop, the park offers breathtaking panoramic views of the nearby mountains and the old gold and copper mining town of Itogon.

Visitors can enjoy the cool climate and capture memorable photos with the stunning backdrop. The park is divided into different sections, including the observation deck, shopping area, dining grounds, and souvenir shops. At the observation deck, visitors can witness the captivating sunrise over the Cordillera mountains and the mining town.

Mines View Park also offers various amenities such as shops selling souvenirs made by local artisans, food stalls offering local delicacies like grilled dried squid and steamed sweet corn, and the opportunity to pose for photos with St. Bernard dogs or horses. It is a must-visit destination for those seeking beautiful views and a taste of the local culture.

How to get there

To reach Mines View Park, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. Take a bus from Manila to Baguio City. Several bus companies offer trips to Baguio from different terminals in Manila. The journey usually takes around 6-7 hours, depending on traffic and road conditions.

2. Once in Baguio, you can take a taxi, jeepney, or ride-sharing service to Mines View Park. It is a well-known tourist spot in the city, so many drivers will be familiar with the location.

By Private Vehicle:

1. Drive from Manila to Baguio City via the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and follow the directions to Mines View Park once in Baguio. Use navigation apps like Waze or Google Maps for the best route based on current traffic conditions.

2. There are parking areas available near Mines View Park where you can park your private vehicle while exploring the park.

Whether you choose public transportation or a private vehicle, these directions will help you reach Mines View Park conveniently in the CAR region.
