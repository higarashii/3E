Sablayan Zipline

The Sablayan Zipline in Occidental Mindoro offers an exciting adventure for thrill-seekers. It boasts one of the longest ziplines in Asia, providing breathtaking views of lush forests and pristine beaches as participants soar through the air. The zipline experience offers an adrenaline rush while showcasing the natural beauty of the surrounding landscape.

The zipline site may offer amenities such as safety gear rental, rest areas with scenic views, and snack bars where visitors can purchase refreshments. Trained staff ensure the safety of participants throughout the ziplining experience, making it suitable for adventurers of all levels.

Accommodations are available in Sablayan town, catering to various budgets and preferences. Visitors can choose from budget hostels to beachfront resorts, providing comfortable stays during their visit. Additionally, activities in the area may include snorkeling, diving, and island hopping, allowing travelers to explore the natural wonders of Occidental Mindoro.

How to get there

Sablayan is accessible by road from major cities in Occidental Mindoro.To reach Sablayan Zipline in Occidental Mindoro from Mindoro, travelers can utilize various transportation modes such as ferries, boats, buses, or vans depending on their preference and availability.

Upon arriving in Sablayan, visitors can hire local transportation like tricycles to reach the zipline site.
