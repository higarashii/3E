Burnham Park

Burnham Park, located at Jose Abad Santos Drive, Baguio City, Benguet, is a renowned urban park in the heart of Baguio City, Philippines. Spanning approximately 32 hectares, Burnham Park offers a wide range of attractions and activities for visitors of all ages. One of its main features is the picturesque Burnham Lake, a man-made body of water where visitors can rent rowboats and enjoy a leisurely ride while taking in the serene surroundings.

Additionally, visitors can explore the rose garden, orchidarium, and picnic grove, providing beautiful natural settings for relaxation and enjoyment. With its central location and diverse attractions, Burnham Park is a must-visit destination for tourists and locals alike.

The park also boasts a variety of amenities such as the Melvin Jones Grandstand, which serves as a venue for major events and concerts, and the athletic bowl, which offers facilities for track and field sports.

How to get there

To reach Burnham Park, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. Take a bus from Manila to Baguio City. Several bus companies operate trips to Baguio from different terminals in Manila. The journey typically takes around 6-7 hours, depending on traffic and road conditions.

2. Upon arriving in Baguio, you can take a taxi, jeepney, or walk to Burnham Park, which is centrally located in the city.

By Private Vehicle:

1. From Manila, drive towards Baguio City via the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and the scenic Kennon Road or Marcos Highway. Once in Baguio City, navigate to Burnham Park using GPS or local directions.

2. There are parking facilities available near Burnham Park where you can park your private vehicle while visiting the park.

Whether you option for public transportation or a private vehicle, these directions will help you reach Burnham Park conveniently in the CAR region.
