Dinosaurs Island

Dinosaur Island is an interactive theme park located in Clark Freeport Zone, Pampanga, Philippines. This unique attraction is designed to educate and entertain visitors about the world of dinosaurs, featuring life-sized dinosaur replicas, interactive exhibits, and themed shows. The park is designed to resemble a prehistoric environment, with lush greenery and landscapes that create a realistic backdrop for the dinosaurs. Visitors can walk through various zones, each offering a different perspective on the age of dinosaurs, from educational presentations to thrilling encounters with animatronic dinosaurs.

A highlight of Dinosaur Island is the Dinosaur World of Fun, where visitors can witness live dinosaur shows and interact with realistic dinosaur models. The park also features the Dino Trail, a guided walk through different eras of dinosaur history, and the Fossil Museum, where guests can learn about paleontology and view fossil replicas.

How to get there

To reach Dinosaurs Island, you can follow these general directions:

To visit Dinosaur Island in Clark Freeport Zone, Pampanga, you can travel by car or use public transportation. If you're driving from Manila, take the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and then the Subic-Clark-Tarlac Expressway (SCTEX). Exit at Clark South and follow signs directing you to the Clark Freeport Zone. Once inside, follow signs to Dinosaur Island, which is located near other attractions like Clark Safari and Adventure Park. For public transportation, you can take a bus to Dau or Angeles City, then transfer to a jeepney or taxi that takes you into Clark and to Dinosaur Island. It's best to check transportation schedules and traffic conditions for the smoothest journey.
