Pukaway cave in coron

Pukaway Cave just recently opened its doors to tourists this 2023. It was previously a private property by a local in Coron who decided to finally share its breath-taking beauty to tourists and visitors. Unlike other caves, this cave did not have that big mouth where people can easily go inside. Instead, it has a very narrow mouth which makes entering the cave a bit challenging. Tourists are encouraged to wear helmets and clog shoes for protection. But there is no need to worry because in just a few steps you are already inside the cave.

How to get there

It is easy to get to Pukaway Cave even without booking a tour with a travel agency. You can go to Pukaway Cave DIY way but you have to take note that this is only recommended if you are traveling with a large group to minimize the expenses. Otherwise, the best option will be to book with a travel agency.
