Hidden Valley Springs

Hidden Valley Springs is a hidden gem nestled in the lush mountains of Calauan, Laguna, Philippines. This natural paradise offers a tranquil and immersive experience in the heart of nature. The highlight of Hidden Valley Springs is its pristine and secluded hot spring pools, which are fed by the surrounding tropical rainforest.

The resort also offers various activities for guests to enjoy, such as bird watching, fish feeding, and butterfly watching. Nature enthusiasts will be delighted by the abundance of flora and fauna that call Hidden Valley Springs home. The resort is committed to preserving the natural environment, making it an eco-friendly destination. It provides a truly enchanting experience that will leave you feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

How to get there

To reach Hidden Valley Springs, you can follow these general directions:

To reach this place Just an hour and a half drive from Manila towards SLEX take Exit 50 towards Batangas and Laguna and head towards Batangas. Take Left Lane when you reach Y intersection. When you reach Y intersection Take lane heading to Alaminos, look out for the caltex station on the left side of the road. Approximately 200 meters from the left side of the station, take the left lane in the Y intersection. From this point follow the road signs going to Hidden Valley springs.

Below are some nearby hotels for you to stay

Hidden Valley Springs Resort

Information:

Located in Calauan, Laguna, this resort is the closest accommodation to Hidden Valley Springs. It offers comfortable rooms surrounded by lush greenery and natural beauty. Guests can enjoy the resort's hot spring pools, hiking trails, and spa services.

The resort also has a restaurant that serves delicious meals. Staying at Hidden Valley Springs Resort allows guests to fully immerse themselves in the tranquil ambiance of the area.

Pandin Lake Eco Park

Information:

Situated in San Pablo City, Laguna, Pandin Lake Eco Park is another nearby option for accommodation. This eco-park offers cottages and rooms with scenic views of the lake. Guests can enjoy activities such as kayaking, swimming, and hiking.

The eco-park also provides delicious meals made from locally sourced ingredients. Staying at Pandin Lake Eco Park offers a unique and immersive experience in the natural surroundings.
