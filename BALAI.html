Balai na Ilagan in Isabela

Nestled in the heart of Isabela Province, Balai na Ilagan beckons travelers with its serene ambiance and warm hospitality. This charming retreat offers a peaceful escape amidst the picturesque landscapes of the countryside, providing guests with a tranquil haven away from the hustle and bustle of city life. Surrounded by lush greenery and towering trees, Balai na Ilagan exudes rustic charm and invites visitors to unwind and reconnect with nature.

Balai na Ilagan features cozy accommodations in traditional Filipino-style cottages, each designed to provide comfort and relaxation. Guests can immerse themselves in the laid-back atmosphere while enjoying modern amenities and personalized service. Whether lounging by the pool, strolling through the gardens, or indulging in delectable local cuisine at the on-site restaurant, visitors are treated to a memorable stay filled with tranquility and rejuvenation.

How to get there

To reach Balai na Ilagan in Isabela, you can follow these general directions:

To reach Balai na Ilagan from Manila, travelers have several transportation options. The most convenient way is by private vehicle, taking the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and continuing onto the Subic-Clark-Tarlac Expressway (SCTEX) and the Tarlac-Pangasinan-La Union Expressway (TPLEX). From TPLEX, take the exit toward Ilagan City, then follow signs leading to Balai na Ilagan. Alternatively, travelers can take a bus from Manila to Ilagan City, then hire a taxi or tricycle to reach the resort.
