Aquino Center and Museum

Located at Hacienda Luisita, San Miguel, Tarlac City, Tarlac, Philippines

The Aquino Center and Museum was established by the Board of Trustees of the then Benigno S. Aquino, Jr. Foundation together with equally committed individuals to enshrine and continuously learn from the valuable lessons of our people’s struggle to restore freedom, democracy, and genuine development. It was formally inaugurated on August 21, 2001, the eighteenth anniversary of Ninoy Aquino’s martyrdom.

Its main feature is the Museum, which houses photos and memorabilia of Ninoy and Cory Aquino. It also chronicles the Filipinos proud recent history: their fight to restore democracy and their shining example of People Power that inspired the world.

The Center also features conference and event facilities that provide an inspiring setting for leadership formation, training, and team building activities; fellowship among corporations, government agencies, and non-government organizations; and other social gatherings

How to get there

The Aquino Center and Museum is located approximately 130 kilometers north of Manila, the capital city of the Philippines. You can travel by private vehicle, public transportation, or organized tour.

By Public Transportation:

1. from Manila, you can take a bus bound for Tarlac City. Several bus companies operate routes to Tarlac, with terminals located in various parts of Metro Manila such as Cubao, Pasay, and Sampaloc.

2. Upon reaching Tarlac City, you can take a tricycle or jeepney to Hacienda Luisita where the Aquino Center and Museum is located. Tricycles and jeepneys are common modes of transportation in the area and can be found near the public market or terminal.

By Private Vehicle:

1. By Private Vehicle, from Manila, take the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and exit at the Concepcion Exit in Tarlac. After the tollgate, turn right onto the MacArthur Highway (AH26).

2. Continue driving straight on MacArthur Highway until you reach Tarlac City. Once in Tarlac City, continue on MacArthur Highway until you see signs for Hacienda Luisita. Turn right into Hacienda Luisita and follow the road until you reach the Aquino Center and Museum.

By following these directions, you should be able to reach the Aquino Center and Museum and explore its exhibits and galleries dedicated to the legacy of the Aquino family and Philippine democracy.
