MAGSANGYAW LAND OF PRAISE

Magsangyaw Land of Praise is located on a mountainous peak, providing a panoramic view of the surrounding green mountains and fields.

The center of attraction at the peak are 3 towering crosses that seemingly guards the whole place and its surroundings.

​

Aside from these crosses, there are structures where you can take photos such as a flying broomstick and a magical carpet where your imagination and creativity can run wild.

How to get there:

From the General Santos Airport (GES), Philippines, 1h 30min by bus ₱110 to YBL Marbel Station Koronadal and 40min by bus ₱59 to Surallah and take to Sitio Kitobo, Tupi.
