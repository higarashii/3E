Sicogon Island

Sicogon Island located in the northern region of Iloilo, which is part of the municipality of Carles, Iloilo, Philippines.

It's' a hidden gem with pristine beaches, beautiful surroundings, and a peaceful atmosphere that draws travelers looking for a place to relax. Sicogon Island, which is reachable by boat from the town of Carles, provides a getaway from the rush of city life with its pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters, luring visitors to relax and rejuvenate amid the beauty of nature.

Sicogon Island is a paradise for both nature lovers and explorers because of its abundant biodiversity and robust marine life. The island provides a wide range of outdoor activities for visitors to discover and enjoy, from hiking through deep woods brimming with exotic flora and wildlife to diving along vivid coral reefs. Immersed in the natural beauty of the Philippines, Sicogon Island offers a memorable experience for those seeking adventure in its pristine wildness or leisure on its pristine shores.

How to get there

To reach Sicogon Island, you can follow these general directions:

The easiest and fastest way to reach Sicogon Island is to take a bus bound for the town of Estancia in Iloilo province, which is at least four hours from the take-off point at Tagbak Terminal in Jaro District in Iloilo City.

Tourists have to be at the Estancia port before 8 a.m. to catch the motorized pumpboat that will take them to Sicogon at P60 per person. The island is about 45 minutes away from Estancia port.
