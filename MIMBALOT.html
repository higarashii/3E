Mimbalot Falls

Mimbalot is a very picturesque small sized falls that is a good place to visit Iligan. It is located at Brgy, Iligan City, Lanao del Norte. It's not too far and there is no entrance fee needed.

Mimbalot Falls is an awesome tourist attraction to visit especially for nature lovers who enjoys the beauty of our God-given environment. Just one of the 39 waterfalls in the City, Mimbalot Falls is part of the "big three" or the "tourism triangle" together with the other two waterfalls mentioned above.

Via habal-habal ride, you'll reach one of the accessible falls in Iligan City, Lanao del Norte. It's clear waters is very enticing to get a quick dip

How to Get there

To reach Mimbalot, you can follow these general directions:

1. Fly or travel to Iligan City: If you're coming from outside Iligan City, you can take a flight to Laguindingan Airport, which is the nearest airport. From there, you can take a taxi or a van to Iligan City. If you're already in the Philippines, you can also take a bus or drive to Iligan City directly.

2. Go to the City Proper: Once you're in Iligan City, head to the city proper where you'll find the public transportation options to Mimbalot Falls. You can take a taxi, a jeepney, or a habal-habal (motorcycle taxi) to reach the falls

3. Travel to Buru-un: Mimbalot Falls is situated in the Barangay Buru-un, which is about 11 kilometers away from the city proper. You can inform your driver or ask for transportation options that go to Buru-un.

4. Arrive at Mimbalot Falls: Once you reach Buru-un, you'll need to find the entrance to Mimbalot Falls. There might be signs or locals who can guide you. The falls are located in a secluded area, so it's important to follow the proper paths and avoid trespassing on private property.
