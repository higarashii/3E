Camp John Hay

Camp John Hay is a renowned tourist spot in Baguio City, Philippines. It is a picturesque destination known for its beautiful surroundings and a wide range of activities. The camp, which was previously a military base of the United States Armed Forces, has now been transformed into a mixed-use development that serves as a tourist attraction and forest watershed reservation.One of the highlights of Camp John Hay is its signature Baguio pines ambiance. The camp is nestled amidst lush greenery, offering a serene and tranquil atmosphere for visitors to enjoy. The cool climate of Baguio City adds to the charm of the place, making it a popular choice for those seeking a break from the hot and humid lowland areas.

Inside the sanctuary of Camp John Hay, there are numerous activities to engage in. Visitors can explore the Bell House Museum and Amphitheater, which is a unique coliseum-style park and a notable landmark within the camp. The Bell House Museum showcases the rich history of the area, while the Amphitheater provides a perfect setting for events and gatherings. Additionally, Camp John Hay offers various recreational opportunities such as hiking, horseback riding, and golfing. The camp also features a wide range of accommodation options, including hotels and cottages, allowing visitors to stay and fully immerse themselves in the beauty of the surroundings.Camp John Hay is located in Baguio City, Philippines. It is situated in the scenic mountains of Baguio, providing visitors with a beautiful natural setting to enjoy.
