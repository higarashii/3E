Cogon Eco-Tourism Park

Cogon Eco Park is located in the barangay of Cogon in the beautiful City of Dipolog, Zamboanga Del Norte. It started as a reforestation project in 1952, covering 344 hectares of land and forest. The beautiful nature of this eco park is very popular tourists destination. It is a nice vacation spot for families and friends who want to spend worthwhile time together. This is also the perfect location for those who want thrilling outdoor activities such as camping, trekking and mountain climbing.

As a vast forest, Cogon Eco Park has various kinds of trees. The most popular species of trees are Acacia, Mahogany and Narra. Of course, you shouldn't miss the Cogon trees – it's the park's namesake after all! For tree huggers, you will surely enjoy pointing at different trees and guessing their names, some of which are Duguan, Tianong, Lumbayao, Yemane, Narig, Yemane, Nato, Lauan and Teak. The office supervise the entrance of tourists to Cogon Eco Park. Staff members warmly welcome people who want to experience the Cogon Jungle Adventure. The building features a stunning pond and garden. Before entering the park, you will already be amazed with its mini zoo composed of exotic species like snakes, hawks, boars, eagles, doves, peacocks, monitors lizards and exotic birds endemic to the area.

The Cogon Jungle Adventure is your ultimate nature experience. You will feel closer to the environment. You might think it's just in stories but you will hear the whisper of the trees and the gentle touch of the wind. Trekking along the forest trails is refreshing and rejuvenating indeed!

How to get there

To reach Cogon Eco-Tourism Park, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. First is booking a flight from Manila to Zamboanga International Airport. Upon arrival at Zamboanga International Airport, you'll find public transportation options like within the airport.

2. Ride a tricycle from your hotel/house in Dipolog to Satellite Market bus terminal, Dipolog City for 7.00 Php. This will take 10 minutes if your hotel is located near the central barangay. From the terminal, ride a bus/jeep/truck bound or that will pass "Diwan" or "Cogon" Dipolog City. "Pagadian City" bound buses also travel via this route. RURAL Co. Bus fare to COGON ECO PARK as of 7/21/2010 is 30Php. Ask the conductor to drop you at the COGON Eco park entrance near the highway. Look for a sign "WELCOME TO COGON ECO PARK" along the highway. There are separate ramp road as ENTRANCE and EXIT that is 50 meters apart. Distance is 42 KM from the main city and travel time is 48 minutes. You can always ride your own car/van to get to the place with no road problems

By Private Vehicle:

1. Travelling using a rented car or privaely own vehicle from nearby towns or cities, you can use online maps or navigation apps to guide you to Cogon Eco-Tourism Park.

2. Renting a car is still the same as commuting. After landing at Zamboanga International Airport, use a navigation app to go there, and it is approximately a 2–3-hour drive.

It's recommended to verify the specific routes and transportation options based on your starting point and current travel conditions. Additionally, always exercise caution and follow local traffic regulations while traveling.
