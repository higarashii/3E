Vera Falls

Vera Falls is a breathtaking natural attraction located in the Philippines. It's renowned for its stunning cascades, crystal-clear pools, and lush surroundings, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventurers alike. The falls offer visitors an opportunity to swim in its refreshing waters, surrounded by the beauty of the tropical landscape. Located in the province of Antique, Vera Falls is often considered one of the hidden gems of the region, as it's nestled amidst verdant forests, requiring a bit of a trek to reach. The journey to the falls is often part of the adventure, as visitors hike through the scenic terrain to reach the cascades. While not as widely known as some other waterfalls in the Philippines, Vera Falls offers a tranquil and picturesque setting for those seeking a more off-the-beaten-path experience. It's a place where visitors can immerse themselves in the beauty of nature, away from the hustle and bustle of city life.

How to get there

LVera Falls is a beautiful destination! To get there, you'll first need to know its location. Vera Falls is located in Barangay Vera, which is in the municipality of Malinao, Aklan, Philippines. Here's a general guide on how to get there:

-Arrive in Aklan: If you're traveling from outside the province, you'll likely arrive in Aklan via air or sea. The main airport serving Aklan is Kalibo International Airport, while there are also sea ports in Caticlan and Kalibo.

-Travel to Malinao: Once in Aklan, you'll need to travel to the municipality of Malinao. You can do this by bus, van, or private car. Buses and vans heading to Malinao are available at the Kalibo International Airport or in Caticlan.

-Reach Barangay Vera: From the town center of Malinao, you'll need to travel to Barangay Vera where Vera Falls is located. You can hire a tricycle or a habal-habal (motorcycle taxi) to take you there. Make sure to inform the driver that you're heading to Vera Falls.

-Hike to Vera Falls: Upon reaching Barangay Vera, you'll need to hike a short distance to reach Vera Falls. The hike usually takes around 15 to 30 minutes depending on your pace. The trail is relatively easy and surrounded by lush greenery.

-Enjoy Vera Falls: Once you've reached Vera Falls, take your time to appreciate its natural beauty. You can swim in the cool waters, take photos, have a picnic, or simply relax amidst the serene surroundings.
