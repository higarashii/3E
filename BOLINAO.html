Bolinao Falls

Bolinao Falls is a serene and picturesque natural attraction located in the municipality of Bolinao, Pangasinan, Philippines. Tucked away amidst lush greenery, the falls offer a refreshing retreat for visitors seeking tranquility and adventure.

The falls cascade down into a pristine pool, creating a stunning backdrop against the surrounding tropical vegetation. The clear, turquoise waters invite visitors to take a refreshing dip or simply relax and enjoy the peaceful ambiance.

Surrounded by verdant forests and vibrant flora, Bolinao Falls provides an ideal setting for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. Visitors can explore the area on hiking trails, admire the beauty of the cascading waters, or simply bask in the natural beauty of the surroundings.

With its untouched beauty and serene atmosphere, Bolinao Falls offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, making it a must-visit destination for those exploring the beauty of Pangasinan.

How to get there

To reach Bolinao Falls, you can follow these general directions:

Start from Bolinao town proper.

Hire a tricycle or motorcycle (habal-habal) near the town center.

Inform the driver you want to go to Bolinao Falls.

The driver will take you on the road leading to Bolinao Falls.

Enjoy the ride until you reach the falls.
