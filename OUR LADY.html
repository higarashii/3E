Our Lady of the Most Holy Rosary Cathedral

The Our Lady of the Most Holy Rosary Cathedral Parish, commonly known as the Dipolog Cathedral, is a Roman Catholic church located in Estaka, Dipolog, Zamboanga del Norte. It was established in 1896 and serves as the seat of the Diocese of Dipolog.

The cathedral features wooden ceilings, bas-reliefs of the four Evangelists, massive adobe stone walls, and a rich history that reflects the region's religious heritage.

The Dipolog Cathedral offers a glimpse into the past and a connection to faith that transcends time and borders. Its rich heritage, religious significance, and architectural beauty make it a must-visit for those exploring Zamboanga del Norte.

How to get there

To reach Our Lady of the Most Holy Rosary Cathedral, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. By Local Buses or Jeepneys, look for local buses or jeepneys that operate within Dipolog City. Board a bus or jeepney heading towards Estaka where the cathedral is located. Inform the driver or conductor that you want to go to the Dipolog Cathedral or Our Lady of the Most Holy Rosary Cathedral.

2. By tricycle, Once you reach Estaka, you can take a tricycle, motorized three-wheeler to the cathedral. Tricycles are readily available near bus stops or other transportation hubs.

By Private Vehicle:

1. If you are renting a car, from Dipolog City, start by heading towards Estaka in Dipolog City. Look for signs indicating the way to the cathedral. The cathedral is located along Padre Ramon St. in Estaka.

2. Once you arrive at the cathedral, find available parking spaces nearby. There may be street parking or designated parking areas around the cathedral.
