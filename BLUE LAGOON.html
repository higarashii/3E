Blue Lagoon

The Blue Lagoon in Pagudpud, Ilocos Norte, Philippines, is a stunning coastal destination known for its crystal-clear turquoise waters and powdery white sand beaches. Nestled along the northern coast of Luzon Island, this idyllic stretch of shoreline offers visitors a tranquil retreat amidst breathtaking natural beauty.

The Blue Lagoon is famous for its serene atmosphere and pristine waters, which are perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing. The calm, shallow bay makes it ideal for families and those looking for a relaxing beach experience. Visitors can also enjoy a variety of water sports such as kayaking, paddleboarding, and jet skiing.

Surrounded by lush greenery and towering coconut palms, the Blue Lagoon provides a picturesque backdrop for leisurely strolls along the shore or picnics on the beach. At sunset, the sky comes alive with vibrant hues, creating a magical setting for romantic walks or simply unwinding as you watch the day fade into dusk. Nearby, you'll find amenities such as resorts, restaurants, and beachfront cottages where you can savor delicious local cuisine and immerse yourself in the laid-back coastal lifestyle. Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, the Blue Lagoon in Pagudpud offers an unforgettable escape into paradise.

How to get there

By Public Vehicle:

To travel from Manila to Evangeline Beach Resort in Ilocos, you can take a bus from terminals like Victory Liner, Partas, or Florida Bus Lines.

The journey typically takes around 8-10 hours, depending on traffic and road conditions. Once you arrive in Ilocos, you can take a tricycle or taxi to reach the resort, which is located in Currimao, Ilocos Norte.

By Private Vehicle:

Car or Motorcycle

If you prefer driving, you can rent a car in Manila and take the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and the Subic-Clark-Tarlac Expressway (SCTEX) until you reach Ilocos.
