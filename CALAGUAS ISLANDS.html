How to get there

To reach Calaguas Islands, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. Take a bus from Manila to Paracale, which is the jump-off point to Calaguas Islands. The bus journey can take around 10-12 hours. From Paracale, hire a tricycle to the fish port and then take a boat to Calaguas Islands. The boat ride from Paracale to Calaguas Islands is approximately 2-2.5 hours.

2. If you are coming from Naga City, you can take a van to Daet and then another van or bus to Paracale. From Paracale, rent a boat to Calaguas Islands. The boat ride from Paracale to Calaguas Islands is around 2-2.5 hours.

By Private Vehicle:

1. If you are traveling by private vehicle to Calaguas Islands, you can drive from Manila to Paracale. The drive can take around 10-12 hours, depending on traffic and road conditions.

2. From Paracale, follow the same route as public transportation by hiring a boat to Calaguas Islands.

These transportation options provide a guide on how to get to Calaguas Islands in Region 5 by both public transportation and private vehicle.
