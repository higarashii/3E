Donsol

Donsol, officially the Municipality of Donsol, is a 3rd class municipality in the province of Sorsogon, Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 50,281 people. Donsol is 66 kilometres from Sorsogon City and 571 kilometres from Manila. Donsol is known primarily for whale watching. Here tourists can interact with whale sharks (butanding in local parlance) by snorkeling, diving, or by simply waiting for them in areas where they come right up to the surface. Donsol has been recognized as the “Whale Shark Capital of the World”.

These gentle giants cruise Donsol waters because their primary food sources, plankton and algae, are abundant in Donsol River, which feeds into Donsol Bay. As of 2023, the World Wildlife Fund - Donsol has identified 43 individual whale sharks that have visited its waters, 16 of which are newcomers to Donsol. Tourists also have to abide by strict rules to ensure that the whale sharks would not be harmed, such as not having more than 6 swimmers per shark, a limited number of boats in the area, a ban on scuba diving in the area, and no feeding of sharks in any way.

How to get there

To reach Donsol, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. Take a bus from Manila to Legazpi City, which is the nearest major city to Donsol. The bus journey can take around 10-12 hours. From Legazpi City, take a van, bus, or jeepney to Donsol. The journey from Legazpi City to Donsol is approximately 1.5 hours.

2. From Legazpi City, you can take a van, bus, or jeepney to Donsol. These public transportation options are readily available and will take you directly to Donsol.

By Private Vehicle:

1. If you are traveling by private vehicle from Manila to Donsol, you can take the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) and Southern Tagalog Arterial Road (STAR Tollway) to reach the Southern Luzon area.

2. From Legazpi City, you can then drive to Donsol. The drive is approximately 1.5 hours and offers scenic views along the way.

These transportation options provide a guide on how to get to Donsol in Region 5 by both public transportation and private vehicle.
