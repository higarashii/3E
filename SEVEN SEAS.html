Seven Seas Waterpark and Resort

Seven Seas Waterpark and Resort is renowned for its thrilling water attractions, which cater to visitors of all ages. From adrenaline-pumping slides and exhilarating rides to relaxing pools and lazy rivers, the waterpark offers a wide range of experiences to suit every preference. Guests can brave the twists and turns of high-speed slides like the Cyclone, plunge down towering water chutes, or simply float along the gentle currents of the lazy river.

Set amidst lush tropical surroundings, Seven Seas Waterpark and Resort provides a picturesque backdrop for relaxation and leisure. Palm-fringed pools, landscaped gardens, and shaded cabanas create an idyllic setting where guests can unwind and soak up the sun. Whether lounging poolside with a refreshing drink or strolling through the resort's scenic pathways, visitors are enveloped in a tranquil oasis of natural beauty.

Throughout the year, Seven Seas Waterpark and Resort hosts a variety of events, concerts, and themed celebrations, adding excitement and entertainment to the guest experience. From live music performances to special holiday festivities, there's always something happening at the resort to delight visitors of all ages.

Overall, Seven Seas Waterpark and Resort in Misamis Oriental offers an unforgettable aquatic adventure for visitors seeking thrills, relaxation, and family fun. With its thrilling rides, family-friendly attractions, tropical ambiance, and modern amenities, the resort provides a memorable escape where guests can create lasting memories with friends and loved ones.


How to get there

To reach Seven Seas Waterpark and Resort, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. Head to Agora Terminal or Eastbound Terminal in Cagayan de Oro City.

2. Look for a bus or jeepney bound for Opol or Iligan City. Confirm with the driver or conductor that they pass by Seven Seas Waterpark and Resort.

3. Alight at the drop-off point nearest to the resort. From there, you may need to take a short tricycle ride to reach the entrance.

By Private Vehicle:

1. If you're traveling by private vehicle from nearby towns or cities, you can use online maps or navigation apps to guide you to Seven Seas Waterpark and Resort.

2. Take the National Highway or Opol-Agora Road, depending on your starting point.

3. Continue on the National Highway until you reach the intersection with Sayre Highway in Opol.

4. Turn left onto Sayre Highway, then continue straight until you reach the resort, which will be on your left-hand side.

It's recommended to verify the specific routes and transportation options based on your starting point and current travel conditions. Additionally, always exercise caution and follow local traffic regulations while traveling.
