Pagayawan Falls

Pagayawan Falls is a hidden treasure within the city of Bacolod, Lanao del Norte. It is located in the province's lush, flourishing forest and is considered to be one of the most breathtaking waterfalls found in the Philippines.

Tourists and locals alike go towards Pagayawan Falls to admire its breathtaking beauty and enjoy an energizing trek adventure going to the falls.

If you're seeking an unforgettable trip at Lanao del Norte, Pagayawan Falls must be at the top of your list of places to visit. The stunning beauty of the falls, its refreshing water, and abundant wildlife make it among the most stunning spots to visit in the Philippines. So, if you're searching for a relaxing and unforgettable holiday, you must visit Pagayawan Falls.

How to Get there

To reach Pagayawan Falls , you can follow these general directions:

1. Once you reach Lanao del Norte, you'll need to travel to Bacolod. You can take a bus, van, or hire a private vehicle from the capital city of Iligan or other nearby towns.

2. In Bacolod, you can hire a habal-habal (motorcycle taxi) or tricycle to take you to the jump-off point of the falls. Seek assistance from the locals or visit the tourism office for accurate directions and guidance.

3. You'll need to make your way to the jump-off point for Pagayawan Falls. Once you reach the jump-off point, you'll embark on a trek to reach Pagayawan Falls. The trek may involve hiking through trails, crossing rivers, and navigating varying terrain.

4. After the trek, you'll arrive at Pagayawan Falls. Take your time to appreciate the natural beauty of the falls, swim in its refreshing waters, relax, and capture memorable photos.
