TAGTUGUNAN WATERFALLS

Tagtuganan Falls has a very deep catchment pool, surrounded by lush forest trees and vines it was a very nice place to stay and relax. Water was clear that day and everyone started to take a dip and swam crossed the deep pool just to be underneath the gushing water of the falls. Some participants did not cross the pool as they do not know how to swim. They just sat on the bank and fresh their body from the stream of cold water coming from the falls.

What a good way to end this adventure. All are refreshed and we trek back to our assembly area where others are waiting for us. In just one day, I visited three beauties of nature – Kabyawan Cave, Pugad Sayaw Cave and the Tugtuganan Falls. I would like to thank all the Barangay officials and the guide who help us make this event successful and to the participants who willingly abide by the rules set by the Officials.

Location : Tagtugonan Falls Gupitan Kapalong Davao Del Norte Philippines.

How to get there:

At the airport, hire a taxi going to Ecoland integrated Bus Terminal (15kms) for a 30-minute ride. From there, catch up a Bus bound for Digos City, General Santos City or Kidapawan (53 km) then get off at the Digos City Bus Terminal; travel time would be less than two hours to get to Tugtugunan Falls.
