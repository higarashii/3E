Poro Point

Poro Point, located in La Union, Philippines, is a picturesque peninsula that boasts stunning natural beauty and a rich historical significance. Nestled along the shores of the South China Sea, the point offers breathtaking panoramic views of the ocean, providing a serene and captivating escape for visitors. The coastal landscape is characterized by rugged cliffs, golden sandy beaches, and crystal-clear waters, making it a haven for nature enthusiasts and beach lovers alike.

The area is home to the Thunderbird Resorts and Casino, a prominent establishment that adds a touch of luxury to the Poro Point experience. With its Mediterranean-inspired architecture, world-class amenities, and a golf course overlooking the sea, Thunderbird Resorts provides a unique blend of relaxation and entertainment. The resort also features a lighthouse that stands as a symbol of Poro Point's maritime heritage, offering a glimpse into the region's seafaring history.

Beyond its natural beauty and modern amenities, Poro Point holds historical significance as a former military base. The point served as a strategic location during World War II and now houses remnants of bunkers and military structures, providing visitors with a glimpse into the area's wartime past. Poro Point seamlessly combines history, nature, and luxury, creating a destination that appeals to a diverse range of interests and offers a memorable experience for those seeking both relaxation and exploration.

How to get there

The Poro Point Lighthouse is just 10 minutes away from San Fernando’s central district, and you can get there either by car or by riding public transportation. The best thing about it? The place has no entrance fee, so anyone can freely walk around and take in the scenery. It’s also close to Thunderbird Resort Hotel, so you won’t have to worry about a place to stay if you want to be near the lighthouse.
