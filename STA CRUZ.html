Sta. Cruz Falls

Sta. Cruz Falls of Brgy Sta. Cruz, Kapatagan, Lanao del Norte is a hidden treasure hidden in the heart of Lanao del Norte. Located in the municipality of Kapatagan and is the perfect place for an adventure or an unforgettable getaway.

Sta. Cruz Falls is an amazing three-tier waterfall, with an overall drop of about fifty feet. It's an impressive cascading waterfall that has the water pouring down the first layer, and then flowing down into the third and second tiers. The waters are clear and cool and the surroundings are serene.

The most appealing aspect of Sta. Cruz Falls is that it's easily accessible. It's only 20 minutes away in the direction of Iligan City and can also be reached by jeepney from the center of Kapatagan. The path to the falls is a well-paved trail that is suitable for hikers and those who do not. When you reach the waterfall, you can admire the view and snap photos of the beautiful scenery.

How to Get there

To reach Sta. Cruz Falls, you can follow these general directions:

1. You can reach Lanao del Norte by air or land travel. The nearest airport is Laguindingan Airport, located in Misamis Oriental.

2. From there, you can take a bus or private vehicle to Lanao del Norte. Once you arrive in Lanao del Norte, you'll need to travel to Tubod.

3. You can take a bus, van, or hire a private vehicle from the capital city of Iligan or other nearby towns.

4. In Tubod, inquire about local transportation options to reach Sta. Cruz Falls. You can hire a habal-habal (motorcycle taxi) or tricycle to take you to the jump-off point of the falls.

5. Once you have arranged transportation, you'll head to the jump-off point for Sta. Cruz Falls.

6. And, once you arrive at Sta. Cruz Falls, take your time to appreciate the natural beauty of the waterfalls. You can swim, relax, or take photos in the area. Follow any safety instructions provided by local authorities and respect the environment.
