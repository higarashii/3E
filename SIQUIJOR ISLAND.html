Siquijor Island

Siquijor Island is located in Circumferential Road,6227 Lalao, San juan, This is a captivating destination located in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines. Known for its enchanting beauty and rich cultural heritage, Siquijor Island offers a unique and memorable experience for visitors. The island is surrounded by pristine beaches with clear turquoise waters, making it a paradise for beach lovers and snorkeling enthusiasts.

Apart from its natural beauty, Siquijor Island is also steeped in folklore and mysticism. It is often referred to as the "Island of Fire" due to the mystical rituals and healing practices that take place here. The island is home to traditional healers known as "mambabarang" or "mananambal" who use herbs, chants, and rituals to cure ailments and ward off evil spirits.
