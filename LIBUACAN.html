Libuacan Cold Spring

Libuacan Cold Spring is a natural gem located in the municipality of Libuacan in the province of Surigao del Sur, Philippines. Libuacan Cold Spring is nestled within a lush tropical forest, offering a serene and refreshing escape from the heat of the day. The spring is fed by cool, clear waters that emerge from underground sources, creating a tranquil oasis surrounded by verdant vegetation. The natural beauty of the area, with its towering trees and vibrant foliage, provides a picturesque backdrop for relaxation and recreation.

Libuacan Cold Spring is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered, offering a blissful escape into the heart of nature's beauty. Whether you're seeking adventure or serenity, this natural wonder has something to offer for everyone to enjoy.

Libuacan Cold Spring offers a tranquil setting for relaxation and rejuvenation. Whether you choose to lounge by the water's edge, bask in the warmth of the sun, or simply close your eyes and listen to the soothing sounds of nature, the spring provides a peaceful retreat from the stresses of daily life.

How to get there

To reach Libuacan Cold Spring, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1.Depart from Manila and head south on the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) towards Batangas City. Upon reaching Batangas City, proceed to the Batangas International Port. From there, take a ferry bound for Surigao City. It's advisable to check ferry schedules in advance and make reservations if necessary.

2. In Tandag City, you can hire a motorcycle taxi (habal-habal) or take a public jeepney heading towards Libuacan. Inform the driver or conductor that you're going to Libuacan Cold Spring. Once you arrive in Libuacan, you may need to walk a short distance from the drop-off point to reach Libuacan Cold Spring. Follow signs or ask locals for directions if needed.

By Private Vehicle:

1. Depart from Manila and head south on the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) towards Batangas City. Upon reaching Batangas City, proceed to the Batangas International Port. From there, take a ferry bound for Surigao City. It's advisable to check ferry schedules in advance and make reservations if necessary.

2. Upon arriving in Surigao City, exit the port area and head south towards Tandag City, the capital of Surigao del Sur. Follow the signs or ask locals for directions to Libuacan Cold Spring in the municipality of Libuacan. Once in Libuacan, continue following the road until you reach the entrance to Libuacan Cold Spring. Parking may be available near the spring for private vehicles.
