Guinsuhotan Cave & Cagnitoan Waterfall

Guinsuhotan Cave & Cagnitoan Waterfall is a natural attraction located in Brgy. Cagnitoan, Macrohon, Southern Leyte, Philippines. It is a hidden gem nestled amidst lush tropical forests, offering visitors a chance to explore a captivating cave system and cool off in the refreshing waters of a scenic waterfall. The cave features stunning rock formations, underground streams, and unique biodiversity, making it a popular destination for adventure seekers and nature lovers alike. The nearby Cagnitoan Waterfall provides a picturesque backdrop for swimming, picnicking, and relaxation, surrounded by pristine natural beauty.

How to get there

To reach Guinsuhotan Cave and Cagnitoan Waterfall in Maasin, Southern Leyte, you have several options. From Maasin City, take a tricycle or habal-habal (motorcycle taxi) to Barangay Cagnitoan, approximately a 20-30 minute ride. Alternatively, you can hop on a public utility vehicle (PUV) like a jeepney or van bound for Macrohon or Sogod and inform the driver to drop you off at Cagnitoan. If you're driving, use GPS or maps to navigate to Barangay Cagnitoan. Once there, ask locals for specific directions as the site may be in a remote area. Be prepared for some walking or hiking as access to the cave and waterfall may involve trekking through trails or rough terrain. Planning ahead and seeking local guidance will ensure a smooth journey to these natural wonders.
