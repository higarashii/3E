How to get there

To reach Santiago City Plaza, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. Begin your journey by booking a flight from Manila to Butuan City. Butuan City is the nearest major city to Cagwait with an airport. From Butuan City, take a bus or van to Tandag City, the capital of Surigao del Sur. The journey takes approximately 4-5 hours.

2. In Tandag City, you'll find options for public transportation, such as buses, vans, or jeepneys, heading to Cagwait. The travel time is around 2-3 hours. Upon reaching Cagwait, you can hire a tricycle or motorcycle taxi (habal-habal) to take you directly to Cagwait White Beach. Alternatively, you can ask for directions and walk if it's within a manageable distance.

By Private Vehicle:

1. Start by heading south from Manila on the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) and continue onto the Southern Tagalog Arterial Road (STAR Tollway). Follow signs leading to Batangas City. Upon reaching Batangas City, proceed to the Batangas Port. From there, you can take a ferry bound for Surigao City. Make sure to check the ferry schedules in advance and book tickets accordingly.

2. Upon arriving in Surigao City, you'll need to travel by road to Cagwait. The journey takes approximately 3-4 hours. You can either drive your vehicle or hire a local transportation service for convenience. Once in Cagwait, follow signs or ask locals for directions to Cagwait White Beach. It's a well-known destination in the area and shouldn't be hard to find.
