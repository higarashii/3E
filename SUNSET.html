Sunset Boulevard

Sunset Boulevard, also known as the Dipolog Sunset Boulevard, is a picturesque esplanade located in Dipolog City, Zamboanga del Norte. It offers stunning views of the Sulu Sea and is particularly famous for its breathtaking sunsets.

The boulevard features a well-maintained promenade with benches, providing an ideal venue for leisurely strolls, relaxation, and entertainment. As the sun paints the sky in warm oranges, pinks, and purples, it's a perfect spot for couples, families, and friends to enjoy quality time together.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful escape or a romantic date, Sunset Boulevard promises a memorable experience. The tranquil sea, warm colors, and gentle breeze create an enchanting atmosphere.

How to get there

To reach Sunset Boulevard, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. By bus, from Dipolog Terminal, ride a bus going to west Zamboanga del Norte and ask the driver to drop you off at the Sunset Boulevard or Foreshore Development and Wellness Center. The boulevard stretches for about 7.6 kilometers, so you can explore different sections along the way.

2. By Air, if you're coming from Manila, you can take a flight to Dipolog City (the capital of Zamboanga del Norte). Cebu Pacific Air offers daily flights from Manila to Dipolog City, which takes approximately an hour.

By Private Vehicle:

1. If you are renting a car, from Dipolog City, start by heading west from the city center and follow Rizal Avenue, also known as the National Highway. Continue on this road until you reach the Sunset Boulevard area. You'll see signs indicating the boulevard's entrance.

2. There are parking areas along the boulevard where you can safely park your vehicle. Just be mindful of any parking fees or regulations.
