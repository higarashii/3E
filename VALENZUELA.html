Valenzuela People's Park

The Valenzuela People's Park, officially the Valenzuela City People's Park and also known to local residents as simply People's Park or VCPP, is an urban community park located in the city of Valenzuela, Philippines.

It is built beside the Valenzuela City Government Center and the Valenzuela Town Center. It is located at MXRC+V29, MacArthur Hwy, Valenzuela, Metro Manila.

People's Park is bounded by two major roads of the city, MacArthur Highway and A. Pablo Street. The park itself is surrounded by various landmarks, government buildings, and shopping centers. The South Supermarket (in front of the People's Park) and the Valenzuela Town Center (between the People's Park and the New City Hall) are the nearest shopping destinations for park visitors. The park is also located beside the Valenzuela City Government Center.

How to Get there

1. Valenzuela City is well-connected to different parts of Metro Manila and neighboring provinces through public buses. You can take a bus from major transportation terminals like Cubao, Pasay or Monumento that has routes going to Valenzuela City. Look for buses with signs indicating Valenzuela or Malinta.

2. If you are coming from other parts of Metro Manila, you can use the MRT, or LRT systems to reach stations near Valenzuela City,s uch as Monumento or North Avenue. From there, you can take a jeepney, bus or other local transportation options
