Camp Phillips is a community situated in Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon. It is also where the Del Monte Pineapple Plantation is located. It has a vast field of pineapples covering around 9000 hectares of land. A wide area of the place is planted with pineapples that stretch from horizon to horizon.

Upon entering Camp Phillips, one would be able to see a big pineapple statue, a perfect spot for picture taking. How to Get there

To reach Camp Phillips, you can follow these general directions:

By Land Transport:

1. From Cagayan de Oro City: Cagayan de Oro City is the closest major city to Camp Phillips. From the city, you can take a bus or van bound for Manolo Fortich, a municipality in Bukidnon where Camp Phillips is located. The journey takes approximately 1 to 2 hours. In Manolo Fortich, you can hire a tricycle or take a jeepney to Camp Phillips.

2. From other locations: If you are coming from other areas, it's best to check bus or van routes that go to Manolo Fortich. You can inquire at local transportation terminals for the most up-to-date information on schedules and fares.
