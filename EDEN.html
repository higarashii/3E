Discover Eden Nature Park

If you want to commune with nature, you have to visit this next destination. Eden Nature Park and Resort in Davao City was named after the famed Biblical Garden. Trying out Eden Nature Park activities is highly recommended because its serenity and tropical forest climate will remove anybody's anxiety from the stress and noise of the busy city. Whether you want to meditate alone or take a break with your family and friends, a tour in Eden Nature Park is worth it.

The Amphitheater, Lola's Garden, the Flower Garden, Plaza Maria, Mayumi's Well, and others are all tourist spots in the nature park..

The nature park is also a resort. They have a 24-hour front desk, an outdoor pool, and buffet breakfast. Their rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV, a wardrobe, and an en-suite bathroom. Staying at the park means direct access to everything it offers, which is a great way to spend time with your loved ones during your trip to Davao.

How to get there

Transportation to Eden Nature Park:

Public Transportation: If you prefer public transportation, you can take a jeepney or bus bound for Toril District from Davao City. Once you reach Toril, you can hire a motorcycle taxi (habal-habal) or a tricycle to take you to Eden Nature Park.

By Private Vehicle:

Private Vehicle or Rental Car: The most convenient way to reach Eden Nature Park is by driving. You can either use your own vehicle or rent a car in Davao City. From Davao City, you'll need to head towards Toril District. Once you reach Toril, there are signs directing you to Eden Nature Park. The park is located approximately 25 kilometers (about 1 hour) away from Davao City center.
