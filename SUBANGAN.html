SUBANGAN MUSEUM

Subangan Museum is located in Brgy. Datu Martin Marundan, Mati City, Davao Oriental.

Its main attraction is the 53-feet long and weighing 20 metric tons (live weight) sperm whale skeleton called Davor. Davor, obviously named after the Province of Davao Oriental, is placed at the center of the museum that will surely greet your entry.

Subangan Museum is an interactive, state-of-the-art museum that highlights treasured relics, interactive visuals, photos, documents, videos and textiles. Its name is derived from the Bisayan word, subang, which means “east.”

How to get there:

From Manila or Cebu going to Davao City. Then ride a bus or van in Ecoland Bus Terminal. Bus ride from Davao City to Mati is around 2.5 to 3 hours. From Mati Terminal, you can ride motorcyle ( Tricycle or Habal2x ) going to Subangan Museum
