Siargao Island

Barangay Santa Fe, General Luna, Surigao del Norte, Begin your expedition on Siargao Island, the "Surfing Capital of the Philippines". Famous for its Cloud 9 surf break, it attracts surfers from around the globe..Siargao is a tear-drop shaped island in the Philippine Sea situated 196 kilometers southeast of Tacloban. It has a land area of approximately 437 square kilometres (169 sq mi).

The east coast is relatively straight with one deep inlet, Port Pilar. The coastline is marked by a succession of reefs, small points and white, sandy beaches. The neighboring islands and islets have similar landforms. Siargao is known as the surfing capital of the Philippines, and was voted t You sent the Best Island in Asia in the 2021 Conde Nast Travelers Readers awards.

Siargao is a famous tourist destination, well-known for its many surfing spots and featured in the film Siargao for such qualities. Surfing is so ingrained in the identity of Siargao, that in 2022, two political families from Surigao Del Norte traded barbs over the cancellation of a national surfing competition hosted on the island.

Habal-Habal (Motorcycle):

1. If you're traveling by private vehicle from nearby towns or cities, you can use online maps or navigation apps to guide you to Siargao Island.

2. If you're traveling solo or in a small group, you can hire local motorcycle drivers to give you a lift or drive them yourself. These motorcycles for hire are locally known as habal-habal. It's a popular mode of transportation in many small islands in the Philippines, especially in the southern provinces.

Mini-Multicab Rental- If you’re only traveling with a small group of three to four, a van rental may not be economical. A good alternative would be renting a mini-multicab. Binggo offers the first and only private mini multicabs for rent in Siargao.

Bulan Villas Siargao

Information:

Located in General Luna, Bulan Villas Siargao is designed using indigenous Filipino materials like wood, nipa, and cogon leaves. It is one of the Bali-like resorts in the Philippines.

Bulan, which means “moon,” depicts how life slows down on the island because of its simplicity and tranquility. This family-owned property transports guests to that place of relaxation where they can forget the busyness of their everyday lives.

Bulan Villas Siargao also has an in-house restaurant that serves Filipino dishes as well as a curated selection of special wine from Sicily. Its location also makes it attractive for those who want to explore the island by foot. It is near the hippest Siargao restaurants, bars and shops in the area, and just a motorbike ride away from the surf spot. It is also around two kilometers away from Guyam island, and seven kilometers from Daku Island.

Siargao Island Villas

Information:

Just 700 meters away from General Luna Beach, Barangay, Tourism Rd, General Luna, 8419 Surigao del Norte. Siargao Island Villas is a 4-star hotel with premium rooms that will make your stay in Siargao a memorable one. From its Premium Beach Suites situated right in front of the vast Siargao coastline to the Garden Suites that give you a laidback island vibe, Siargao Island Villas lets you experience the island with either a scenic view or a tranquil escape.

The Premium Beach Suites are fully furnished with an indoor hot and cold shower, private bathroom, air conditioning, mini-bar, and a private safety deposit box. Garden Suites are either Premium or Deluxe.

Premium garden suites can accommodate up to five people (with extra cost) and can either be on the upper or lower story. It comes with its own balcony too as well as amenities similar to the Premium Beach Suites. The Deluxe Garden Suites has a one queen-sized bed and similar amenities except for the mini bar.

Siargao Bleu Resort & Spa

Information:

Located on Tourism Road, Brgy. Catangan, General Luna, Just a stone’s throw away from Cloud 9 sits Siargao Bleu Resort & Spa, the islands’ only spa resort. It currently has 40 luxury rooms which come in three types: Floating Cottages with its own private cottage by the pool, stone tub, a king or two queen beds, and mini bar; Garden De Luxe with a ground floor garden room, a queen size bed, and mini bar; and The Terraces located on the second floor with its own veranda, twin beds, and mini bar. Guests have personal butlers upon their requests too.

Its amenities are top-notch too including its world-class restaurant serving the freshest produce from the island. The restaurant is open from 6 am to 10 pm every day. To cap off a day filled with adventure, the resort’s beach bar offers signature cocktails with chill music on the background.

Other features of the resort include an indoor gym for fitness buffs and the Bleu Spa which offers various massage services such as foot massage, thermal massage, stone massage, and Ventosa cupping, among others by highly trained therapists. The resort also arranges free activities such as yoga and movie nights.
