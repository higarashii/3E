Dao Dao Island

Dao Dao Island, nestled in the Pangapuyan Group of Islands off the coast of Zamboanga del Sur is a serene escape boasting pristine beaches and clear waters.

The island offers a picturesque journey through coastal vistas and turquoise seas. Its powdery white sand beaches and lush greenery create an ideal setting for swimming, snorkeling, and relaxation. This hidden gem in the Sulu Sea appeals to nature lovers and those seeking tranquility away from the mainland.

Dao Dao Island's untouched beauty invites exploration of its marine life through snorkeling and leisurely walks along the shore. Interacting with the friendly locals and indulging in fresh seafood add to the island's charm, offering visitors a genuine and rejuvenating experience amidst a pristine tropical setting.

How to get there

To reach Dao Dao Island, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. Reach Pagadian City by bus or plane from major cities like Manila or Cebu. From Pagadian City, take a bus or van bound for Malangas. The travel time is around 2-3 hours.

2. At Malangas Port, take a ferry or motorboat to Dao Dao Island. The boat ride typically takes around 30-45 minutes depending on sea conditions. Make sure to check the ferry schedule beforehand.

By Private Vehicle:

1. Start by driving from Pagadian City towards Malangas Port. Once you reach Malangas Port, you can park your vehicle securely at designated areas or in a paid parking lot if available.

2. From Malangas Port, arrange for a ferry or motorboat to take you to Dao Dao Island. Private boats can also be hired for more flexibility in timing.
