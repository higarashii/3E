Loboc River Cruise

The Loboc River Cruise is located in the town of Loboc, situated in the province of Bohol, Philippines.

The Loboc River Cruise is a popular tourist attraction in Bohol that offers visitors a unique experience of enjoying a delicious meal while cruising along the serene and picturesque Loboc River.

During the cruise, guests are treated to a traditional Filipino feast known as "floating restaurant" while being entertained by local musicians and dancers performing on board. The cruise also provides an opportunity to witness the stunning natural beauty of Bohol's countryside and its rich cultural heritage.

The Loboc River Cruise is not only a dining experience but also an opportunity to appreciate the local culture and traditions while enjoying the tranquil ambiance of the river that flows through lush green landscapes and quaint villages.

How to get there

To reach Loboc River Cruise, you can follow these general directions:

From Tagbilaran City (the main entry point to Bohol via airport or ferry)

By Car/Taxi : The most convenient way is to hire a car or taxi. It takes about 30 minutes to reach Loboc, depending on traffic.

By Bus: You can catch a bus from the Dao Terminal in Tagbilaran heading towards Loboc. Buses that go to towns further east like Bilar or Carmen will also stop in Loboc. This is a cheaper option but may take longer.

From Panglao Island

By Car/Taxi : Traveling by car or taxi from Panglao to Loboc takes about 45 minutes to an hour. You would head to Tagbilaran first, then continue to Loboc.

By Bus : First, take a jeepney or tricycle to Tagbilaran, then switch to a bus headed for Loboc at Dao Terminal.

From Other Parts of Bohol : If you're traveling from other parts of Bohol, such as Anda or Carmen, direct routes via bus or hired vehicle can be taken to reach Loboc, with buses generally stopping in the town center.

In Loboc : Once in Loboc, head to the Loboc Tourism Complex, where the river cruises depart. This area is well-marked and known locally, so any local transport method can get you there easily.
