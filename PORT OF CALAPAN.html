Port of Calapan

The Port of Calapan serves as a vital transportation hub, connecting Oriental Mindoro to other ports across the Philippines. It is a bustling gateway for travelers and goods alike, with ferries and boats departing regularly to various destinations. The port area reflects the dynamic maritime culture of the region.

The port offers amenities such as waiting lounges, ticketing counters, and cargo services for the convenience of travelers. Additionally, various dining options and shops may be available nearby, allowing visitors to purchase essentials or enjoy a meal before their journey.

Accommodations near the Port of Calapan cater to the needs of travelers, ranging from budget hotels to mid-range resorts. Visitors can find comfortable stays within close proximity to the port, making it convenient for transit passengers. Activities in the area may include ferry rides to nearby islands, city tours of Calapan, and exploring local markets.

How to get there

To reach the Port of Calapan from Mindoro, travelers can take a ferry from Batangas Pier to Calapan. Ferries operate regularly throughout the day, with varying departure times.

The journey typically takes around 1.5 to 2 hours and offers scenic views of the surrounding islands.
