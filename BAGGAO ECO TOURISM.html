Baggao Eco-Tourism Zone

Baggao Eco-Tourism Zone in Baggao, Cagayan, offers a remarkable blend of natural wonders and cultural experiences. One unique feature of this zone is its rich biodiversity, boasting diverse flora and fauna native to the region. Visitors can explore lush forests teeming with endemic species, providing a captivating glimpse into the area's ecological significance.

Baggao Eco-Tourism Zone is home to the stunning Dummun Falls, a picturesque cascade surrounded by verdant foliage, offering a serene and refreshing retreat for nature enthusiasts. What sets Dummun Falls apart is its secluded location, providing a sense of exclusivity and tranquility to visitors seeking a peaceful getaway.

For those interested in cultural immersion, the zone offers opportunities to engage with the indigenous communities residing in the area. Visitors can partake in traditional activities, such as basket weaving and folk dances, allowing them to gain insight into the local way of life and heritage.

How to get there

To reach Baggao Eco-Tourism Zone, you can follow these general directions:

By Air:

The nearest major airport is Tuguegarao Airport (TUG), also known as the Tuguegarao Domestic Airport. From there, you can either rent a vehicle or take a bus or van to Baggao.

By Land:

Baggao is accessible by land transportation from various points in Luzon. From Tuguegarao City, you can take a bus or van bound for Baggao. The journey typically takes around 2 to 3 hours, depending on road conditions and traffic.

By Private Vehicle:

If you're driving from Manila or other parts of Luzon, you can take the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and the Subic-Clark-Tarlac Expressway (SCTEX) until you reach the Pan-Philippine Highway (also known as Maharlika Highway). From there, you'll head north towards Tuguegarao City, then follow signs or directions to Baggao.

Public Transportation:

Buses and vans regularly ply the route between Tuguegarao City and Baggao. You can catch these vehicles at the Tuguegarao City terminal. Once in Baggao town proper, you can inquire about transportation options to reach the specific sites within the Baggao Eco-Tourism Zone.
