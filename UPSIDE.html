Upside Down Museum

Is located at Boom na boom grounds ccp complex, Roxas blvd, Pasay, 1300 Metro Manila.

Upside Down Museum is the newest craze in Mega Manila /Philippines. It features a massive upside down house - complete from Living room, dining,kitchen,bedroom,office and bathroom.

It also features a real steel bridge with real SUVs(cars) - all upside down. It also features illusions that are never before seen in the Philippines. The challenge is to do crazy poses for a photokeeper. The largest interactive social media museum in Amsterdam, where you make the rules. It's like we threw a museum, an amusement park, a nightclub, a few perfect backdrops and some magic mushrooms in a giant blender to create the ultimate Dutch experience.

How to Get there

1. In order to go to the upside down museum from Cubao, one would need to take the MRT or LRT and get off at the Cubao station

2. From there, they would need to take a jeepney or bus bound for Katipunan and get off at the Ateneo de Manila University

3. The upside down museum is located at the basement of the university's shopping center.
