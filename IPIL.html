Ipil Rotunda Obelisk

Ipil Rotunda Obelisk is the most famous landmark in Zamboanga Sibugay.

A prominent landmark in Ipil, Zamboanga Sibugay, this high stone pillar serves as a unique marker that distinguishes the municipality from others in the province. Built in 2015, the obelisk is a tribute to the lives lost during the 1995 attack when 200 Abu Sayyaf militants struck Ipil.
