Kaamulan Park

Make the most of your Bukidnon adventure at Kaamulan Park, a government-owned park and zoo in Malaybalay City.

The name "Kaamulan" comes from the tribal word "amul," which means "gather" in their language. It is located at J Barroso Drive, Malaybalay, Bukidnon. This park is a haven for nature lovers seeking activities such as jogging, walking, and meditation.

Also, the park provides a perfect setting to relax and reconnect with nature with well-maintained trails, picnic areas, and a swimming pool. Aside from being a sanctuary, it is also a zoo where you can encounter fascinating wild animals like tigers, lions, and even the rare Cassowary bird.

How to Get there

To reach Kaamulan Park, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. From Cagayan de Oro, you can take a bus or a van at the Agora Terminal bound for Malaybalay City..

2. Once you arrive in Malaybalay City, you can take a tricycle or a motorcycle taxi (habal-habal) to Kaamulan Park. The park is a popular tourist destination, so most locals should be familiar with its location.

By Private Vehicle:

1. From Cagayan de Oro, take the National Highway heading south towards Valencia City in Bukidnon.

2. Continue on the National Highway until you reach Malaybalay City, the capital of Bukidnon.

3. In Malaybalay City, follow the signs leading to Kaamulan Park. It is located near the city center and is well-known, so it should be easy to find.
