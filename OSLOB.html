Oslob Whale Sharks

The Oslob Whale Sharks attraction is located in Barangay Tan-awan, Oslob, Cebu, Philippines. It is situated approximately 10 kilometers away from the center town of Oslob. Whale shark watching in Oslob-Cebu is an unforgettable experience that attracts visitors from all over the world. This unique adventure allows you to swim alongside the gentle giants of the sea in their natural habitat. The whale sharks in Oslob are majestic and friendly, allowing tourists to get close and personal with them.

In addition to the whale shark watching, the area is also known for its stunning natural beauty. The greenery surrounding the area adds to the overall allure of the experience. There are also tour packages that include a visit to the nearby Kawasan Falls in Badian. This all-day adventure package offers a unique blend of thrill and tranquility, making it a must-try for anyone visiting Cebu. So, whether you're an adrenaline junkie or a nature lover, whale shark watching in Oslob-Cebu is a bucket-list-worthy experience.
