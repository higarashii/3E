Kalanggaman Island

This bird-shaped island sits off the coast of Palompon, Leyte. Kalanggaman Island is best known for its 795-meter-long white sandbar that along with its surrounding waters, makes it one of the best Philippine sceneries. Featuring powdery sand and surrounded by aqua water, it has easily become one of the best sandbars in the Philippines and among the top tourist spots in Visayas. While one of the favorite activities here is to stroll along the soft sandbar, you can also plunge into the waters to discover the marine ecosystem hidden just beneath the waves. Visitors may also rent cottages along the shore, where you can relax after soaking up some sun and enjoying the cool waters.

How to get there

Fly from Manila to Ormoc.
Go to the van and bus terminal near Ormoc City Park from Ormoc Airport.
Take the van (Php 120) going to Palompon, Leyte.
It usually takes an hour and a half to two hours to reach Palompon Leyte.
From Palompon, go to the Eco-Tourism Office.
Register and rent a boat going to Kalanggaman Island.
