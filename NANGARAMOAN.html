Nangaramoan Beach

Nangaramoan Beach is situated in the barangay (village) of Nangaramoan, which is part of the municipality of Santa Ana in the province of Cagayan. It is located on the northeastern coast of Luzon, facing the Pacific Ocean.

Nangaramoan Beach is known for its pristine white sand shores, clear turquoise waters, and scenic coastal landscapes. The beach offers a tranquil and idyllic setting, perfect for relaxation and leisure activities. Visitors can enjoy swimming, sunbathing, beachcombing, and picnicking against the backdrop of stunning natural beauty.

While Nangaramoan Beach maintains its natural charm, there are limited facilities available on-site. Visitors may find basic amenities such as shaded picnic areas, open-air cottages, and restroom facilities. However, it's advisable to bring essentials such as food, water, sunscreen, and beach gear for a comfortable stay.

How to get there

To get to Nangaramoan Beach in Santa Ana, Cagayan, Philippines:

1. Travel to Santa Ana by bus or private vehicle.

2. From Santa Ana town center, take a tricycle or drive to Nangaramoan Beach.

3. Follow signs or ask locals for directions.

4. The beach is accessible via a paved road with parking available nearby.
