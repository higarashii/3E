Enchanted Kingdom

Enchanted Kingdom, It is affectionately known as EK, is a world-class theme park located in the RSBS Boulevard, San Lorenzo Rd, South, Santa Rosa, 4026 Laguna. Established in 1995, it's the country's first and only theme park of its scale. Spread over 25 hectares, EK boasts of seven themed zones each inspired by various elements of magic and enchantment.

Beyond the rides, Enchanted Kingdom is also well-known for its live entertainment. From musical performances, fireworks displays, to character meet-and-greets, there's always something exciting happening around every corner.
