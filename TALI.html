Tali Beach

Guis-Guis Tali Beach is a popular destination located in Nasugbu, Batangas, Philippines. It's known for its stunning coastline, clear waters, and beautiful sandy shores. The beach is a favorite spot for swimming, snorkeling, and other water activities. Many visitors also come to Guis-Guis Tali Beach to relax and enjoy the picturesque surroundings. If you're planning a trip there, be sure to bring your beach gear and sunscreen for a day of fun in the sun.

The beach boasts pristine white sand and clear blue waters, creating a picturesque setting that attracts visitors seeking natural beauty and tranquility. Visitors can enjoy a variety of water activities such as swimming, snorkeling, and beachcombing, making it an ideal destination for both relaxation and adventure.

How to get there

To reach Guis-Guis Tali Beach in Nasugbu, Batangas, Philippines, you can follow these steps:

From Metro Manila:

If you're starting from Metro Manila, you can head to one of the bus terminals such as Buendia or Cubao. Look for buses bound for Nasugbu, Batangas. There are several bus companies that operate this route, including DLTB Co., Jam Liner, and Celyrosa Express, among others.

Bus Ride:

Purchase a ticket for Nasugbu, Batangas. Buses usually have regular schedules throughout the day. The travel time from Metro Manila to Nasugbu can vary depending on traffic conditions, but it typically takes around 3 to 4 hours.

Arrival in Nasugbu:

Once you arrive in Nasugbu town proper, you'll need to take additional transportation to get to Guis-Guis Tali Beach. Tricycles and jeepneys are common modes of transportation for reaching Tali Beach from Nasugbu.

Tricycle or Jeepney Ride:

You can hire a tricycle or look for jeepneys bound for Tali Beach. These vehicles are usually available near the bus terminal or other transportation hubs in Nasugbu. Inform the driver that you're headed to Guis-Guis Tali Beach. They should be familiar with the destination.

Travel Time:

The travel time from Nasugbu to Guis-Guis Tali Beach is relatively short, usually around 15 to 30 minutes depending on traffic and the specific location of your accommodation.

Accommodations and Facilities:

Once you arrive at Guis-Guis Tali Beach, you can check into your accommodations if you've made prior arrangements. There are resorts and rental properties available for visitors. Explore the area and enjoy the beach, amenities, and activities available at Guis-Guis Tali Beach.

Return Trip:

When it's time to return to Metro Manila or your next destination, you can follow the same steps in reverse, taking a tricycle or jeepney back to Nasugbu town proper and catching a bus bound for Metro Manila.
