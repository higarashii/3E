Mt. Batulao

Mount Batulao is a popular hiking destination in Nasugbu, Batangas, known for its stunning landscapes, picturesque views, and diverse hiking trails that cater to hikers of various skill levels.Mount Batulao offers hikers a rewarding outdoor experience with its scenic trails, rolling hills, and panoramic views of Batangas and nearby provinces. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned hiker, the mountain provides a range of trails to suit different preferences and skill levels.As you ascend Mount Batulao, you'll be treated to breathtaking views of lush greenery, ridges, and the surrounding countryside, making it a photographer's paradise. The mountain features multiple trails, with the Old Trail and New Trail being the most commonly used. The Old Trail offers a more challenging and scenic route, while the New Trail is considered more beginner-friendly.

Reach the summit of Mount Batulao to enjoy panoramic views of nearby peaks, Taal Lake, and the coastal towns below, providing a sense of accomplishment and a picture-perfect backdrop.Camping overnight is a popular option for those who wish to witness a stunning sunrise or stargaze from the mountain. Just make sure to adhere to Leave No Trace principles and secure necessary permits if required.Embarking on a hike to Mount Batulao promises a memorable outdoor adventure filled with scenic vistas, physical challenges, and a sense of accomplishment as you conquer the trails and summit. Remember to take in the beauty of the surroundings, savor the journey, and stay safe throughout your hiking experience at Mount Batulao!

How to get there

Take a bus (DLTB) from Buendia Bus Terminal bound for Nasugbu, Batangas, and go down to KC Hillcrest. (Travel Time: 3 Hours) Take a trike/walk from the highway going to the jump-off/registration area.
