Catandayagan Falls

A beautiful waterfall located in Bato, Catanduanes, Catandayagan Falls is surrounded by lush greenery

Catandayagan Falls is a stunning waterfall located in the municipality of Sagnay, Camarines Sur, Philippines. It's known for its natural beauty and tranquil surroundings, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The waterfall cascades into a refreshing pool, perfect for swimming and cooling off on a hot day. Surrounding the falls are lush greenery and towering trees, creating a picturesque backdrop for relaxation and exploration. Visiting Catandayagan Falls offers a chance to immerse yourself in the beauty of nature and enjoy a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Catandayagan Falls is a hidden gem tucked away in the forests of Camarines Sur, offering visitors a serene and unspoiled natural experience. To reach the falls, adventurers typically embark on a trek through the jungle, crossing streams and navigating rocky terrain. The journey to Catandayagan Falls is an adventure in itself, as you immerse yourself in the sights and sounds of the wilderness.

How to get there

To reach Cantandayagan Falls, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

Take a bus bound for Sagnay town. You can find these buses at the Naga City Central Bus Terminal. Make sure to ask the conductor or driver to drop you off at the nearest point to Catandayagan Falls.Once you arrive in Sagnay town, you can hire a tricycle or motorcycle to take you to the jump-off point for Catandayagan Falls.

By Private Vehicle:

Take the Pan-Philippine Highway (AH26) southbound towards the direction of Sagnay town.Once you reach Sagnay town, you can ask locals for directions to the jump-off point for Catandayagan Falls.Depending on road conditions and accessibility, you may be able to drive closer to the jump-off point or park your vehicle in designated areas.
