Bukidnon Provincial Capitol

Found along the way to Kaamulan Park, the Bukidnon Provincial Capitol building is one of the major landmarks in Malaybalay. It is located at San Victores St, Malaybalay, 8700 Bukidnon. It is an interesting cultural photo stop if ever you're in the area. Built in 1933 and funded by the Americans, the building features colonial-inspired architecture and stands as one of the more outstanding kapitolyos built during the Commonwealth period.

Bukidnon Provincial Capitol in Northern Mindanao, Philippines is a picturesque destination surrounded by lush greenery and towering mountains. The tranquil atmosphere and cool climate make it a perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Explore the vibrant culture, indulge in delicious local cuisine, and uncover the rich history of this charming province.
