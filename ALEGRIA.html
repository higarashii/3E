Alegria Beach

Alegria Beach, Santa Monica, Surigao del Norte, Philippines. Alegria Beach is located in Alegria, a municipality in the province of Cebu. Alegria Beach is an unspoiled beach in Alegria, Cebu. It has a laid back atmosphere intended for those seeking real sanctuary from the crowds. The beach extends a warm welcome to all guests with its crystal clear waters and sandy beach, inviting guests to relax, dine, swim, and party with loved ones.

Guests can begin a tour at Alegria Beach by diving into the waters, followed by some exciting action-packed beach activities like snorkeling, diving, and more. The beach is adjacent to the Saint Joseph Parish Church and other tourist spots in Cebu. The white sandy beaches of Alegria Beach provide a soft and inviting landscape for beachgoers to unwind and soak up the sun. The crystal clear waters offer a refreshing oasis, perfect for swimming or simply taking a leisurely dip. As you bask in the serene atmosphere, you’ll be surrounded by the breathtaking beauty of the beach and its surroundings.

Whether you prefer to lounge on the beach, take a leisurely stroll along the shore, or engage in water activities such as snorkeling or kayaking, Alegria Beach offers something for everyone. The serene and unspoiled atmosphere provides a sense of tranquility, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in the natural surroundings and create lasting memories.

How to get there

To reach Alegria Beach, you can follow these general directions:

From General Luna, Alegria Beach is about 1.5 hours drive. You can rented mopeds and set off on a day-trip from the south to the north. The drive is incredibly scenic and you will be constantly surrounded by coconut palms. The drive up will take you past Tayangban Cave, Magpupungko Rock Pools and through the town of San Isidro.

If you have time you can to stop off at the caves or rock pools. Just before you reach Alegria Beach the road will take you directly past TakTak falls. On your drive up you can decided to stop at the falls, which is the only waterfall on Siargao Island. Also It is a few kilometers away from the city so you’ll have to ride a bus or van to get there. You can visit Alegria Beach anytime of day, however it’s recommended to visit during the summer months, from March to May.
