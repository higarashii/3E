Katibawasan falls

One of the most beautiful natural landmarks in Camiguin, Philippines, is Katibawasan Falls. This magnificent waterfall, which is situated in the municipality of Mambajao, is located at a height of roughly 70 meters and tumbles down to a glistening pool below, providing tourists with an amazing spectacle. The falls, which are surrounded by dense tropical vegetation, provide a tranquil atmosphere that makes them the ideal place to unwind and revitalize while taking in the splendor of nature. on a hot days in particular, the waterfall's cool mist is a delightful sensation.

In addition to being a beautiful place to visit, Katibawasan Falls is significant to the local community culturally.

How to get there There are several convenient routes to reach Katibawasan Falls in Camiguin traveling by air You can land at Camiguin Airport, which is situated in Mambajao, the island's capital municipality.

To get to the falls from there, you can rent a motorbike or a tricycle if you're already on the island, another option is to go to Katibawasan Falls from your lodging by multicab or tricycle.

The actual town of Mambajao is located about 5 kilometers southeast of the falls.
