Sputnik Aviation has launched a new initiative called the "Airline Experience" at their flight simulator facility located on Pioneer St., Mandaluyong City. The company, which was established during the pandemic, has evolved from a flight simulator streaming platform to the Philippines' first recreational flight simulator facility.

The airline experience is open to everyone, even those without a general knowledge of the ins and outs of aviation. The simulator at AAG mimics the feels and the handling of an A320 on a 1:1 scale. Pilots are using it to train for their type-rating.
