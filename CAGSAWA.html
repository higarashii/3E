Cagsawa Ruins

Information:

The Cagsawa Ruins are located in Albay Province, Philippines. They are the remnants of the 17th-century Cagsawa Church, which was destroyed during the eruption of Mayon Volcano in 1814. The ruins are a popular tourist destination, offering a dramatic view of the volcano in the background. The site serves as a reminder of the region's volatile geological history and attracts visitors interested in history, culture, and natural phenomena.

The Cagsawa Ruins have become a popular tourist attraction, drawing visitors from around the world. Tourists can explore the ruins, learn about the history of the site through informational displays, and take in breathtaking views of Mayon Volcano in the background. Many visitors also opt to take guided tours of the surrounding area or participate in outdoor activities such as hiking and ATV rides.

How to get there

To reach the Cagsawa Ruins, you'll likely need to travel to Albay Province in the Philippines, specifically to the municipality of Daraga, where the ruins are located. Here's how you can get there:

By Air: The nearest major airport is Legazpi Airport (also known as Bicol International Airport), which serves flights from Manila and other cities in the Philippines. Once you arrive at Legazpi Airport, you can take a taxi or a tricycle to Daraga, where the Cagsawa Ruins are situated.

By Land: If you're traveling from other parts of the Philippines, you can take a bus or van to Legazpi City, the capital of Albay Province. Several bus companies operate routes to Legazpi from Manila and other major cities. From Legazpi City, you can take another bus, jeepney, or tricycle to Daraga.

Local Transportation: Once you're in Daraga, you can hire a tricycle or a jeepney to take you to the Cagsawa Ruins. Tricycles are a common mode of transportation in the Philippines and are readily available for hire. Make sure to negotiate the fare with the driver before starting your journey.

Self-Drive: If you prefer to drive yourself, you can rent a car in Legazpi City and drive to the Cagsawa Ruins. The ruins are located along the Maharlika Highway, making them easily accessible by road. Just be aware of local traffic rules and road conditions.

Tour Packages: Many travel agencies in Legazpi City offer tour packages that include visits to the Cagsawa Ruins along with other attractions in Albay Province. Consider booking a guided tour if you prefer a hassle-free experience with transportation and itinerary planning taken care of.
