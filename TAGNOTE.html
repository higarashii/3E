Tagnote Falls

Tagnote Falls, located in Remedios T. Romualdez, Agusan del Norte, is a picturesque two-tiered waterfall that captivates visitors with its natural beauty. Nestled at the foot of a majestic mountain, this hidden gem offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.
The falls cascade down a 20-foot high rock cliff, creating a mesmerizing spectacle. The water flows into a 10-square-meter wide basin, which is approximately 3 feet deep. The basin serves as a refreshing plunge pool, inviting visitors to take a dip and cool off. Lush trees surround the falls, providing ample shade for those seeking respite from the sun. Flat boulders nearby serve as perfect picnic spots or resting grounds for weary travelers.

How to get there

To reach Tagnote Falls, embark on a 30 to 45-minute walk from Barangay San Antonio. The journey takes you through creeks, lush forests, and a short uphill stretch enveloped by Mother Nature. Whether you’re a nature enthusiast, an adventure seeker, or simply looking for a tranquil spot, Tagnote Falls is a must-visit destination in Agusan del Norte
