Seco Island

Seco Island (seco means “elbow” in the local language) lies in the Sulu Sea, 50 kilometers west of Panay Mainland. The island is great for kite-surfing, as strong wind blows almost all year round. It’s just a tiny island but has great kiting conditions. Seco Island is located offshore of Tibiao, Antique.

How to get there

There are only 2 jump off points to Antique – Iloilo City and Caticlan. It is around 172 kilometers from Iloilo City and 93 kilometers from Caticlan in Aklan province. The bus ride is around 4 hours from Iloilo (fare is around Php 200.00) and 2 hours from Caticlan (fare is around Php 100.00). There are two-way motorboat rates going to Seco Island, Php 2,000.00 good for 2 passengers and Php 10,000.00 good for at least 10 persons.
