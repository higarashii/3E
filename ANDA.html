Region VII: Anda White Beach

Anda White Beach Resort Anda White Beach Resort is a small and tranquil island getaway that offers you a white sand beach, crystal clear tropical waters and endless sun. This is an authentic tropical paradise with well-manicured grounds and breathtaking views all around, either from the comfort of your room or from the seaside swimming pool as well as spectacular dining.

An emerging favorite tourist destination, Anda Beach is around 110 KM away from Tagbilaran. The beach is located on the eastern coast of the island in the small town after its namesake. The three-kilometer stretch of powdery white sand is often likened to the shores of the world-famous Boracay Island. Along with its smooth shore, Anda Beach also boasts clear.

Anda is also famous for its deep blue cave pools such as Combento, Cateres, Kaligoon, Tiburako, East Coast and Cabagnow. The last cave pool is said to be the “biggest and most beautiful.” These cave pools also provide great conditions for swimmers.

The resort's remote location allows you to immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Bohol, where pristine white sands meet crystal-clear waters. Although the airport is situated 160 minutes away, the journey to Anda White Beach Resort is well worth it, as you'll be rewarded with a slice of paradise upon arrival.

How to get there

To reach Anda White Beach, you can follow these general directions:

Travelers can reach Anda Beach by taking a van or bus from Dao Termina in Tagbilaran City. You can also drive a private car from the city and pass through Bohol Circumferential Road. Otherwise, you can book tour packages that include a stop at the beach for a hassle-free trip:

Getting to Anda Beach requires a little effort, but trust us. It’s worth every bit of it. The easiest way to reach Anda Beach is by flying into Panglao Airport in Bohol. You can hire a private car or take a van to Anda from there. The journey takes around two hours, but the scenic drive through the lush countryside makes it a pleasant experience.

Alternatively, you can take a ferry from Cebu to Tagbilaran and proceed to Anda by land. Once you arrive in Anda, you’ll be greeted by friendly locals who are more than happy to assist you in getting to your accommodation.

If you're traveling by private vehicle from nearby towns or cities, you can use online maps or navigation apps to guide you to Anda White Beach.

It's recommended to verify the specific routes and transportation options based on your starting point and current travel conditions. Additionally, always exercise caution and follow local traffic regulations while traveling.
