TINOTO WALL AND CORAL GARDEN

Tinoto Wall and Coral Garden, situated in Maasim, Sarangani, is a popular diving and snorkeling site.

It boasts vibrant coral formations, diverse marine life, and pristine waters. Snorkelers and divers can explore the colorful coral gardens, encounter various species of fish, and marvel at the underwater beauty.

The wall diving experience offers a thrilling adventure, as divers explore the vertical coral wall teeming with marine biodiversity. This spot is a paradise for underwater enthusiasts and nature lovers.

HOW TO GET THERE?

To get to Tinoto Wall and Coral Garden in Maasim, Sarangani, you can follow these directions:

1. By Air: The nearest airport to Tinoto Wall and Coral Garden is General Santos International Airport (GES). From the airport, you can take a taxi or a hired car to Maasim, which is approximately a 30 to 45-minute drive away.

2. By Land: If you are coming from General Santos City, you can take a public utility van or jeepney bound for Maasim. These vehicles can be found at the public transport terminal in General Santos City. The travel time from General Santos City to Maasim is around 30 to 45 minutes.

If you are coming from other parts of Mindanao or the Philippines, you can take a bus bound for General Santos City. From General Santos City, you can follow the same route as mentioned above.

3. By Private Vehicle: If you have your own vehicle or are renting one, you can drive to Tinoto Wall and Coral Garden. From General Santos City, take the General Santos City-Maasim Road and follow the signs leading to Tinoto Wall and Coral Garden. The travel time is approximately 30 to 45 minutes, depending on traffic conditions.

Once you arrive in Maasim, Tinoto Wall and Coral Garden are easily accessible. You can ask the locals for directions or follow the signs leading to the site. It is always recommended to plan your trip in advance and check for any road closures or travel advisories before heading to Tinoto Wall and Coral Garden.
