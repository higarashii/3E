Cascarro Islet

Cascarro Islet, nestled in the serene waters of Romblon, Philippines, is a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. This idyllic islet boasts pristine white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters teeming with marine life, and lush tropical foliage, making it the perfect destination for a tropical getaway.

Cascarro Islet offers a range of amenities to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay for visitors. From cozy beachfront cottages to luxurious resorts, accommodations cater to various preferences and budgets. Amenities include beachside dining options serving delicious local cuisine, water sports equipment rentals for snorkeling and kayaking, and guided tours to explore the island's natural wonders.

Accommodation options on Cascarro Islet range from charming beachfront bungalows to eco-friendly lodges, providing guests with a true island experience. Activities abound, with opportunities for sunbathing, swimming, and beachcombing along the pristine shores. Adventure seekers can embark on snorkeling or diving excursions to discover vibrant coral reefs and colorful marine life, while cultural enthusiasts can explore nearby villages and learn about the rich heritage of Romblon.

How to get there

To reach Cascarro Islet from Romblon, travelers can hire a local boat or join a guided island-hopping tour. The journey takes approximately 20-30 minutes, depending on the weather and sea conditions.

Once at Cascarro Islet, visitors can explore its pristine beaches, go snorkeling or diving, and enjoy picnics amidst the beautiful scenery.
