Tumalog Falls

Tumalog Falls is located in the town of Oslob, Cebu, Philippines. It is roughly 170 kilometers from Cebu City, typically requiring a drive of about 3 to 4 hours depending on traffic conditions. Tumalog Falls is one of the natural attractions that make Oslob a popular destination, aside from its famous whale shark watching activities.

Tumalog Falls is known for its stunning beauty, characterized by its tall but gentle cascade over a wide, amphitheater-like formation covered with lush, mossy vegetation. The waterfalls create a thin, veil-like drizzle as they descend, which has a soothing, almost ethereal appearance. The pool at the bottom is shallow and clear, inviting visitors to wade and enjoy a refreshing dip.

The area surrounding the falls is relatively undeveloped, adding to its charm and the feeling of being close to nature. The approach to the falls requires a short walk from the parking area, which can be a bit steep, though motorbike services are available to take visitors closer to the site.

Tumalog Falls is particularly popular because it offers a serene and picturesque setting ideal for relaxation and nature photography. It's a must-visit for those traveling to Oslob, especially for those looking to unwind after an early morning of whale shark watching.

How to get there

To reach Tumalog Falls, you can follow these general directions:

1. Start from Cebu City: Begin your journey on the South Road Properties (SRP) road heading towards Talisay.

2. Continue on Cebu South Road: Follow the Cebu South Road all the way through the towns of Minglanilla, Naga, San Fernando, and Carcar. This route will take you along the eastern coast of Cebu.

3. Pass through the Towns of Argao and Dalaguete: After Carcar, continue south to pass through Argao and Dalaguete. These are scenic towns with coastal roads that offer views of the sea and the mountains.

4. Proceed to Oslob: Continue driving south until you reach the town of Oslob. This portion of the drive will take you close to the southeastern tip of Cebu Island.

5. Turn for Tumalog Falls: Once in Oslob, look for signage that directs you to Tumalog Falls. You will turn off the main highway and onto a smaller road leading up to the falls. This road can be steep and narrow.

6. Arrive at Tumalog Falls: The road will lead you to a parking area. From there, you can either walk down to the falls or take a motorcycle (habal-habal) that can be rented from locals.

Alternative Transportation: If you're not driving, you can take a bus from the South Bus Terminal in Cebu City to Oslob. Tell the conductor you wish to visit Tumalog Falls so they can drop you off at the correct junction. From there, you can hire a motorcycle to take you to the falls.

Remember to check local travel advisories and road conditions before setting off, as this can impact your travel plans.
