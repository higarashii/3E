La Trinidad Strawberry Farm

La Trinidad Strawberry Farm is a popular tourist destination located in the town of La Trinidad, Benguet, Philippines. It is just a few kilometers away from Baguio City and is known for its vast fields of strawberries.The farm spans over 79 hectares and is home to a variety of strawberry species. Visitors can partake in the unique experience of strawberry picking, where they can roam the fields and handpick their own strawberries to take home. The strawberry picking season typically runs from November to May, with the peak harvest time being from March to April.

Aside from strawberries, the farm also grows other crops such as lettuce, broccoli, and cauliflower. There are also flower gardens that add to the picturesque scenery of the place.The La Trinidad Strawberry Farm is not just a place for tourists but also serves as a research and training center for the Benguet State University. The university uses the farm for research purposes and to train students in the cultivation of strawberries and other highland vegetables.
