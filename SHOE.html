Shoe museum

The Marikina Shoe Museum in Marikina City,Philippines, is a renowned destination showcasing the city's rich heritage and craftsmanship in shoe-making. The museum features a wide collection of shoes worn by prominent figures, including former Philippine presidents, world leaders, celebrities, and athletes.

It also displays various styles of footwear, historical artifacts, and information about the shoe industry's evolution in Marikina .

How to get there

To reach Shoe museum, you can follow these general directions:

1.Public Transportation: You can take public transportation options such as jeepneys, buses, or UV Express vans depending on your location in Metro Manila. These modes of transportation typically have routes that pass through Marikina City.

2.Route Information: Ensure you are on a route that will take you to Marikina City. You can ask the driver or conductor to confirm if their route passes through Marikina.

3.Driving Directions: If you're driving to the museum, you can input the museum's address into your GPS device or smartphone navigation app. The address is J.P. Rizal Street, Barangay San Roque, Marikina City.
