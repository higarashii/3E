SAN PEDRO METROPOLITAN CATHEDRAL

Located in 3J75+WMV, San Pedro Street, Poblacion District, Davao City, Davao del Sur, Philippines

Modernist Catholic basilica with a curved, concrete facade, ornate altar & separate bell tower. San Pedro Cathedral is not only the oldest church in Davao City, but also the city's premier historical landmark. It was named after San Pedro (Saint Peter), the patron saint of Davao. The original structure was built in 1847 during the arrival of the Spanish Conquistadors, led by Don Jose Uyanguren.

The Metropolitan Cathedral of San Pedro, also known as the San Pedro Cathedral Parish or Davao Cathedral, is a Roman Catholic cathedral located at Barangay 2-A, Poblacion District, Davao City, Philippines. The cathedral, dedicated to Saint Peter, is the ecclesiastical seat of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Davao.
