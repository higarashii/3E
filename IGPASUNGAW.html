Igpasungaw Falls

Igpasungaw Falls has four or more series of waterfalls but only three to four waterfalls are possible to be visited. Igpasungaw Falls is not a single waterfall. Rather, it is a series of nine cascading waterfalls, some of which are yet to be explored. The waterfalls and the river itself are fueled by rainwater and hidden springs far beyond the mountains.

How to get there

The town of Sebaste, Antique is only two towns apart from Tibiao. So, if you’re within the Antique province, you can just take a bus going north, Kalibo. Travel time may only take less than an hour. If you will be coming from the outside of Antique province, particularly in Kalibo or Caticlan, both in Aklan, there’s a bus or van bound to San Jose, Antique. Its route includes Sebaste, Antique. Travel from Kalibo or Caticlan going to Sebaste town center may take two to three hours. Expect a fare of at least P 90 to P100.
