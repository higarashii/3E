FREEDOM PARK

The Freedom Park in Tagum City, Davao del Norte, is a public park that serves as a recreational area and a venue for various events and gatherings. It is a significant landmark in the city, offering green spaces for leisure activities, jogging tracks, playgrounds for children, and spaces for relaxation. The park often hosts cultural events, concerts, and celebrations, particularly during holidays and festivals. It's a favorite spot among locals and visitors alike for unwinding and enjoying outdoor activities in the heart of Tagum City.
