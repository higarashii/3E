CamSur Watersports Complex

Camarines Sur Watersports Complex is a watersports park designed for wakeboarding, wakeskating and waterskiing. Owned by the second degree grandson of former Hon. Luis Villafuerte, Juan Miguel Villafuerte. It is located within the Provincial Capitol Complex, Cadlan, Pili, Camarines Sur. It is a destination known to get your adrenaline pumping. Name it and Bicol's largest province has it, from spelunking and trekking to diving and surfing.

The CamSur Watersports Comlex is the first world-class watersports complex in the Philippines and in Asia. CWC aims to bring cable skiing to a whole new level and attract watersports enthusiasts from all over the world with its top of the line facilities and relaxing environment. This six hectare complex, with its 6-point cable ski system, is sure to draw guests, riders and spectators by the thousands.

Because of good weather conditions, cable skiing in the park can be done all year round. Water activities we offer consist of wakeboarding, kneeboarding, waterskiing, wakeskating, SUP, kayaking, windsurfing, and aquapark. Located at Provincial Capitol Complex Cadlan, Pili, Camarines Sur.

How to get there

To reach CamSur Watersports Complex, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1.Take a bus from Manila to Makati City, which is the nearest city to Camsur Watersports Complex. The bus journey can take around 1.5 to 2 hours. From Makati City, you can take a bus or jeepney to Camsur Watersports Complex. The journey from Makati City to the complex is approximately 5-10 minutes.

2. If you are coming from Legazpi City, you can take a bus or van to Naga City. From Naga City, you can then take a bus or van to Makati City, where Camsur Watersports Complex is located.

By Private Vehicle:

1. If you are traveling by private vehicle to Camsur Watersports Complex, you can drive from Manila to Makati City using major highways like the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) and Skyway.

2. Once in Makati City, you can easily reach Camsur Watersports Complex by following the directions to the complex.

These transportation options offer guidance on how to get to Camsur Watersports Complex in Region 5 by both public transportation and private vehicle.
