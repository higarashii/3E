Laiya Beach

Laiya Beach is a beautiful beach located in San Juan, Batangas, Philippines. It is known for its long stretch of white sand and clear waters. Due to its proximity to Manila, it has become a popular destination for both locals and tourists.

Laiya Beach offers a variety of beach resorts where you can enjoy a relaxing vacation by the sea. Some of the popular beach resorts in Laiya include Laiya Beach Resort, Acuatico Beach Resort, La Luz Beach Resort, Virgin Beach Resort, and Paseo Verde Beach Resort. The beach also offers activities such as banana boat rides and jet skiing. It's the perfect place to unwind, have fun, and enjoy the beauty.
