Nayong Pilipino Clark

Nayong Pilipino Clark is a cultural and heritage park located within the Clark Freeport Zone in Pampanga, Philippines. It serves as a unique representation of the Philippines' rich cultural history and traditions. The park is designed to showcase the country's diverse heritage, with replicas of historical landmarks, traditional Filipino architecture, and exhibits depicting various cultural elements. Visitors can explore different zones representing the nation's regions, learn about Philippine history, and experience traditional Filipino arts, crafts, and performances.

In Nayong Pilipino Clark, you'll find scale models of significant historical sites like the Banaue Rice Terraces, Barasoain Church, and the Rizal Shrine, among others. The park also features gardens, museums, and themed areas where traditional dances, music, and crafts are presented. It's an ideal destination for educational trips, family outings, and tourists interested in immersing themselves in Filipino culture.
