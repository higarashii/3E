DAHICAN BEACH

Dahican Beach in the Philippines is a breathtaking natural paradise featuring long stretches of white sand and crystal-clear waters.

It is renowned as a destination for surfers and skimboarders, thanks to its consistent waves that attract water sports enthusiasts.

Aside from water activities, tourists can also appreciate the beach's natural beauty, swim, and observe local fishermen who showcase traditional fishing techniques. Explore the surrounding attractions, such as the enigmatic Dead City.

How to get there

Has to fly to Davao City, then take either a bus or a van going to Mati (travel time: from 3-6 hours! ... vans are definitely faster) and, from the bus or van terminal there, take a motorcycle ("habal-habal") to the beach. It would take you around 15-20 minutes to reach it.

Costa Lucas

Information:

Costa Lucas , nestled in the picturesque town of Mati in the Philippines, is a pristine stretch of coastline known for its powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters. Spanning several kilometers, the beach offers a plethora of activities for its visitors, including swimming, sunbathing, beach volleyball, and picnicking, ensuring a fun-filled day out for everyone.

Costa Lucas Beach has many amenities, including lifeguards on duty, bathroom access, and it's also pet-friendly, allowing dogs.

However, visitors should note that facilities for individuals with disabilities, nudist areas, camping options, and surfing opportunities are not available here.
