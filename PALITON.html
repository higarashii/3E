Paliton Beach

Paliton Beach is located in the southern part of Siquijor Island, Situated on the northeastern part of San Juan and almost at the western extremity of the island. Also known for its pristine waters, white fine sand beach, and tall coconut-trees which all contribute to complete summer feels. People often call this beach as the “Mini Boracay of Siquijor”. Locals even sometimes make a sand castle for tourists’ photo ops. Also, you can take a picture while being on the Amazing Coconut Tree.

In addition, The beach is famous for its glistening white sands and palm tree-fringed shoreline. It's considered one of the best beaches in Siquijor. It's also a popular place for w You sent watching the sunset, with many people claiming it offers the best sunset view on the island. There are beach chairs available if you are not a fan of sitting on the sand. One of the best things about Paliton Beach is the amazing view of the ocean. The picturesque scene will make you fall in love even more with Siquijor.
