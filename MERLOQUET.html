Merloquet Falls

Located in Barangay Sibulao, Merloquet Falls stands proud near the city’s boundary with Zamboanga Sibugay, approximately two hours away from downtown Zamboanga City. It is a two-tiered drop but its main attraction is the beautiful curtain waterfalls on its base, approximately 10-meter high and 15-meter wide stair-like wall to a shallow pool, definitely one-of-a-kind in the Philippines. The upper tier is a shorter drop and sloped diagonally, approximately 5-meter high and 10-meter wide.

Merloquet Falls may fall short on grandeur compared with other waterfalls in Mindanao but its uniqueness is a charmer. Despite its exposure in the past years, this spot is still in its raw state, and less crowded and busy compared with other known waterfalls in the country. Sit on its natural steps and enjoy the natural back massage of its gushing cold waters or avail of the rappelling experience offered by Zamboanga based adventure companies to best enjoy Merloquet Falls.

How to get there

To reach Merloquet Falls, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. If you're coming from outside Zamboanga City, your first step is to travel to the city using public transportation. You can take a bus, van, or plane depending on your location. From Zamboanga City, head to the bus terminal or van terminal that serves routes going south. Look for vehicles bound for Barangay Sibulao, as this is the nearest barangay to Merloquet Falls.

2. Inform the driver or conductor that you're going to Merloquet Falls in Barangay Sibulao. They should be familiar with the destination and can drop you off at the nearest point. Once you're dropped off, follow the trail leading to Merloquet Falls. The trail may be relatively short but can be challenging, so wear appropriate footwear and take necessary precautions.

By Private Vehicle:

1. Use a map or GPS to plan your route to Merloquet Falls. The falls are located approximately 2 hours away from Zamboanga City proper. You'll need to drive southward towards Barangay Sibulao, where the falls are situated. From Zamboanga City, take the highway going south. Follow signs or directions towards Barangay Sibulao. The road may pass through various towns and villages along the way. Once you reach Barangay Sibulao, ask locals for directions to Merloquet Falls. There should be signs or markers indicating the way to the falls.

2. Upon arrival at Merloquet Falls, there may be designated parking areas where you can safely park your vehicle. Be prepared to pay a small parking fee. Depending on where you park, you may need to hike a short distance to reach Merloquet Falls. Follow the trail leading to the falls, and be sure to wear appropriate footwear for walking on uneven terrain.
