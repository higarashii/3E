MAITUM BEACH

Maitum Beach, located in Maitum, Sarangani, is a scenic beach destination known for its pristine waters and stunning coastal landscapes.

The beach offers a tranquil atmosphere, making it a perfect spot for beach strolls, swimming, and enjoying the beautiful surroundings.

Visitors can relax on the sandy shore, take a dip in the clear waters, or simply soak up the sun while enjoying the picturesque views. The beach offers a serene escape and a chance to connect with nature.

HOW TO GET THERE?

To get to Maitum Beach in Maitum, Sarangani, you can follow these directions:

1. By Air: The nearest airport to Maitum Beach is General Santos International Airport (GES). From the airport, you can take a taxi or a hired car to Maitum, which is approximately a 1 to 1.5-hour drive away.

2. By Land: If you are coming from General Santos City, you can take a public utility van or jeepney bound for Maitum. These vehicles can be found at the public transport terminal in General Santos City. The travel time from General Santos City to Maitum is around 1 to 1.5 hours.

If you are coming from other parts of Mindanao or the Philippines, you can take a bus bound for General Santos City. From General Santos City, you can follow the same route as mentioned above.

3. By Private Vehicle: If you have your own vehicle or are renting one, you can drive to Maitum Beach. From General Santos City, take the General Santos City-Maitum Road and follow the signs leading to Maitum Beach. The travel time is approximately 1 to 1.5 hours, depending on traffic conditions.

Once you arrive in Maitum, Maitum Beach is easily accessible. You can ask the locals for directions or follow the signs leading to the beach. It is always recommended to plan your trip in advance and check for any road closures or travel advisories before heading to Maitum Beach.
