How to get there

To reach Dakak Park and Beach Resort, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. The fastest way is to take a flight from Manila to Dipolog City, which is approximately 14 kilometers away from Dapitan City. There is also a route by sea. Commercial fast crafts have the route from Dapitan to Cebu via Dumaguete and Tagbilaran.

2. Take a bus from Dipolog Airport to the main highway. From Dapitan City, hire a habal-habal (motorcycle taxi) or a tricycle to take you to Dakak Beach Resort.

By Private Vehicle:

1. Travelling using a rented car or privaely own vehicle from nearby towns or cities, you can use online maps or navigation apps to guide you to Dakak Park and Beach Resort.

2. After landing at Dipolog Airport, drive to the main highway, use a navigation app to go there, or ask some locals about the location of Dakak Park and Beach Resort.

It's recommended to verify the specific routes and transportation options based on your starting point and current travel conditions. Additionally, always exercise caution and follow local traffic regulations while traveling.
