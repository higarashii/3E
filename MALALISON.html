Malalison Island

Situated away of the Philippine coast of Antique, Malalison Island is a treasure awaiting discovery.It is located in Culasi in the Antique Province, on the island of Panay in the Philippines.

Malalison possesses a tranquil charm that draws tourists from near and far with its immaculate white sand beaches, clear turquoise waters, and lush rolling hills. The island, also called Mararison, is home to a friendly and hospitable community that reflects the island's rich cultural legacy in addition to its stunning natural beauty.

Arriving at Malalison Island is like stepping into a timeless paradise. Malalison offers a peaceful getaway from the bustle of contemporary life, whether you're relaxing on the beach, hiking up its mild hills for panoramic views, or getting to know the local way of life. Malalison Island's simplicity, tranquility, and natural charm attract visitors to relax, re-establish a connection with the natural world, and form priceless memories amid its picturesque surroundings.

How to get there

To reach Malalison Island, you can follow these general directions:

Take a plane to Caticlan or Kalibo. Afterward, take a bus or van to San Jose and get off at the Culasi Bus Stop. Walk to the Tourism Office and make a reservation for a boat ride to Mararison Island.
