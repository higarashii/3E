Pasig Cathedral Museum

Nestled within the historic confines of Pasig Cathedral, the Pasig Cathedral Museum stands as a testament to the city's vibrant past and enduring legacy. Housed within the hallowed walls of the cathedral, this museum offers visitors a fascinating glimpse into the diverse tapestry of Pasig's cultural heritage.

Beyond its impressive collection, the Pasig Cathedral Museum itself is a work of art. Housed within a beautifully restored colonial-era building, the museum's architecture is a testament to Pasig's rich architectural heritage. From its ornate facades to its majestic interiors, every corner of the museum exudes a sense of grandeur and historical significance, transporting visitors to a bygone era of elegance and charm.

More than just a repository of artifacts, the Pasig Cathedral Museum serves as a hub of learning and inspiration for visitors of all ages. Through its carefully curated exhibits and educational programs, the museum seeks to foster a deeper appreciation for Pasig's cultural heritage and promote a sense of pride and identity among local residents. Whether you're a history enthusiast, a student eager to learn, or simply a curious traveler, there's something for everyone to discover and explore at the Pasig Cathedral Museum.

How to get there

To reach Pasig Cathedral Museum, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. Take the LRT Line 1 and get off at the Central Terminal Station or Recto Station. From there, look for jeepneys bound for Pasig. Make sure they pass through Pasig City Hall or the Pasig City Plaza. Alight at Pasig City Plaza. The Pasig Cathedral is located nearby, within walking distance.

2.If you're closer to the MRT Line 3, take it and get off at the Ortigas Station. From Ortigas Station, you can either walk to EDSA and ride a jeepney going to Pasig or take a short taxi ride to Pasig City Hall or Pasig City Plaza.

3.If you're coming from Quezon City, look for jeepneys bound for Pasig or Shaw Boulevard. Ask the driver if they pass by Pasig City Hall or Pasig City Plaza. Alight there, and the Pasig Cathedral is within walking distance.

By Private Vehicle:

1. Use a navigation app or GPS device to set your destination to Pasig Cathedral Museum. Head towards EDSA or C5 Road, depending on your location.

2.Take EDSA or C5 Road going south, depending on which route is more convenient for you.

3.Look for signs leading to Pasig City Hall or Pasig City Plaza as you approach Pasig City.

4.Once you reach Pasig City Hall or Pasig City Plaza, find parking in nearby parking lots or designated parking areas. The Pasig Cathedral Museum is within walking distance from Pasig City Hall or Pasig City Plaza.

It's recommended to verify the specific routes and transportation options based on your starting point and current travel conditions. Additionally, always exercise caution and follow local traffic regulations while traveling.
