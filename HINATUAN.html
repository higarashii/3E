Hinatuan Enchanted River

Situated in the province of Surigao del Sur, this remarkable watercourse stands as a testament to the majesty of nature and the enduring allure of exploration.

This pristine river, adorned with a combination of cerulean and turquoise color, captivates the senses of everyone who come across its ethereal beauty. Its crystal-clear waters reveal a mesmerizing underwater landscape, where limestone formations and vibrant marine life coexist in harmony.

In addition to it being stunning to look at, locals have spun stories about Hinatuan Enchanted River, filling its waters with tales of mystical creatures and ancient protectors. These narratives add an extra sprinkle of magic to an already enchanting spot.

How to get there

To reach Hinatuan Enchanted River, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. There is no direct connection from Manila to Enchanted River. However, you can take the train to Baclaran LRT, take the walk to Victory Liner, take the bus to NAIA Terminal 2, take the walk to Manila airport, fly to Butuan, take the taxi to Butuan, take the bus to San Francisco, then take the taxi to Enchanted River.

2. It takes approximately 33h 42m to get from Manila to Enchanted River, including transfers and costs ₱3,300 - ₱4,700.

By Private Vehicle:

1. If you're using a private vehicle take the Pan-Philippine Highway (AH26) going southward from Manila. Pass through provinces such as Quezon, Camarines Norte, Camarines Sur, Albay, and Sorsogon until you reach Matnog, Sorsogon. From Matnog, take the RORO (Roll-on/Roll-off) ferry to Samar. Once in Samar, continue driving through Samar and Leyte until you reach Southern Leyte. Take another RORO ferry from Southern Leyte to Surigao City in Mindanao.

2. From Surigao City, take the Surigao-Davao Coastal Road going southward. Pass through the towns of Carrascal, Cantilan, and Tandag until you reach Hinatuan. Once you're in Hinatuan town, follow signs directing you to the Enchanted River. It's approximately a 30-minute drive from the town proper to the Enchanted River.
