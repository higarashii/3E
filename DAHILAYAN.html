Dahilayan Adventure Park & Pine Forest

Dahilayan Adventure Park, now popularly known as "Dahilayan" is one of the best places to experience the most fun and family-friendly activities in Bukidnon. It is located at Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon, Philippines.The home of one of the longest dual zipline in the Philippines one can enjoy the 840-meter zip line and other amenities in the area.

The adventure park continues to offer a growing number of exhilarating attractions like the Razorback Mountain Coaster (Philippines' first alpine coaster), the Skytower Base Jump (the highest in the country), the Flying Lizard Canopy Glider, and more.

If you want to take it more relaxed, there are nature trails where you can enjoy the scenic views through the pine-covered Dahilayan Forest.

The park offers a wide range of attractions, such as the Forest Luge, an exciting go-cart toboggan ride with breathtaking park views. Visitors can also go on ATV rides through a 3.2 KM forest trail, while the hanging bridge offers panoramic vistas unique to Bukidnon.

How to Get there

To reach Dahilayan Adventure Park, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. If you prefer public transportation, you can take a bus or van from Cagayan de Oro City to Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon.

2. At the Agora Terminal in Cagayan de Oro City, look for buses or vans bound for Manolo Fortich.

3. Inform the driver or conductor that you're heading to Dahilayan Adventure Park and Pine Forest, and they will drop you off at the park's entrance or nearby.

By Private Vehicle:

1. If you have a private vehicle or plan to rent one, you can use GPS navigation or follow the road signs.

2. The park is located in Barangay Dahilayan, Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon, Philippines. You can use online mapping services like Google Maps or Waze to get specific directions based on your starting point.
