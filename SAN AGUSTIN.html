SAN AGUSTIN BOTANICAL PARK

San Agustin Botanical Park sit on the opposite side of the town going to Mawab, Compostela Valley Province. If Energy Park is a lowland, this park is a highland. The park is planted with different varieties of Asian trees. San Agustin Botanical Garden, located in the heart of Tagum City, is a lush oasis that showcases the rich biodiversity of the region. Spanning several hectares, the garden is meticulously landscaped with a diverse array of plant species, including native flora, ornamental plants, and exotic tropical specimens. Visitors to the San Agustin Botanical Garden can wander along meandering pathways that wind through themed gardens, each highlighting different aspects of horticulture and ecological conservation. From vibrant flower beds to tranquil ponds and shaded groves, there are plenty of opportunities to immerse oneself in nature and discover the beauty of the botanical world.

In addition to its aesthetic appeal, the garden serves as an educational resource, offering information about plant species, gardening techniques, and environmental conservation. It provides a serene setting for leisurely walks, picnics, and photography, making it a popular destination for families, nature enthusiasts, and tourists alike. Throughout the year, the San Agustin Botanical Garden hosts various events, workshops, and botanical exhibitions, providing opportunities for visitors to learn, engage, and connect with the natural world. Whether seeking relaxation, inspiration, or knowledge, the garden offers a tranquil retreat where visitors can escape the hustle and bustle of urban life and reconnect with the beauty and wonder of the natural world.
