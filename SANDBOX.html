Sandbox at Alviera

SandBox in Alviera is an adventure park located in Porac, Pampanga, Philippines. It is part of the larger Alviera development, a mixed-use estate featuring residential, commercial, and recreational spaces. SandBox is designed to cater to adventure seekers and outdoor enthusiasts, offering a range of thrilling activities for visitors of all ages.

The park is set within a sprawling landscape and provides a unique blend of natural beauty and man-made attractions, creating an ideal setting for family outings and team-building events. One of the key features of SandBox is its extensive adventure courses and activities. The park offers attractions like the Giant Swing, which swings riders up to 10 meters high, and the Aerial Walk Challenge, a series of obstacle courses suspended in the air.

How to get there

To reach Sandbox at Alviera, you can follow these general directions:

To get to SandBox in Alviera, Pampanga, you can drive or take public transportation. If you're coming from Manila, the most direct route is to take the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and exit at the Angeles City exit, then take the Subic-Clark-Tarlac Expressway (SCTEX) toward Porac. Once you exit at Porac, follow the signs to Alviera, where SandBox is located. If using public transport, you can take a bus to Angeles City, then a jeepney or tricycle to Porac, where SandBox is situated. Be sure to check local transportation schedules and road conditions for the smoothest journey.
