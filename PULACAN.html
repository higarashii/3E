Pulacan Falls

Pulacan Falls is a captivating natural wonder located in the town of Labangan, Zamboanga del Sur.

The falls cover an area of approximately 400 square meters and stand at a height that allows water to flow down through different terraces and pools. Unlike standard waterfalls with strong downstream currents, Pulacan Falls resembles a painting, with gentle flowing waters creating a mesmerizing effect.

Its weathered rocks and white cascading water give it a unique charm. It is surrounded by lush vegetation and trees that make you forget about city life.

Pulacan Falls serves as the source of water for the Labangan Irrigation System. It nourishes vast tracts of farmland in the region, making it essential for agriculture. Additionally, the falls’ serene environment has led to its designation as a site for camps and jamborees.

How to get there

To reach Pulacan Falls, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. Take a flight from Manila to Pagadian Airport in Zamboang City after heading to the Integrated Bus Terminal in Pagadian City. Take any bus bound for Ozamiz via Osmena (approximately 14 kilometers). Ask the bus conductor to drop you off at Camp Major Cesar Sang-an or directly at Pulacan Falls if they are familiar with it.

2. From Ozamiz City: Take a bus bound for Pagadian and alight in Pulacan (travel time is around 2 hours). From Dipolog City: Take a bus bound for Pagadian via Osmena (approximately 180 kilometers away from Pulacan).﻿

By Private Vehicle:

1. Travelling using a rented car or privaely own vehicle from nearby towns or cities, you can use online maps or navigation apps to guide you to Pulacan Falls.

2. Renting a car is still the same as commuting. After landing at Pagadian Airport, drive to Ozamiz, or directly straigth to Pulacan Falls just use a navigation app to go there.

It's recommended to verify the specific routes and transportation options based on your starting point and current travel conditions. Additionally, always exercise caution and follow local traffic regulations while traveling.
