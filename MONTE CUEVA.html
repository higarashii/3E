Monte Cueva Shrine

Monte Cueva Shrine is a sacred pilgrimage site situated atop a hill in Barangay Asuncion, Maasin City, Southern Leyte, Philippines. The shrine is renowned for its serene ambiance, scenic surroundings, and religious significance. It serves as a place of worship and spiritual reflection for locals and visitors alike. At the shrine's summit stands a towering cross, symbolizing faith and devotion, while its chapel houses religious artifacts and statues. Visitors can climb the stairs leading to the hill's peak to enjoy panoramic views of Maasin City and its picturesque landscapes.


How to get there

Monte Cueva Shrine is easily accessible from Maasin City proper. Visitors can take a tricycle or jeepney ride to Barangay Asuncion, where the shrine is located. From there, it is a short hike or drive up the hill to reach the shrine's entrance. Private vehicles can also access the shrine, with parking available near the site.
