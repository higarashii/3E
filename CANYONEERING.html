Canyoneering Badian

Badian is located in the south west of Cebu, a popular island in the central Visayas of the Philippines. Badian Canyoneering in Cebu is an outdoor recreational activity that mainly involves traveling through canyons by various methods such as hiking, walking, rappelling, jumping, swimming, and rock climbing.

canyoneering in the municipality of Badian has two options. First one is the downstream canyoneering. This will start from the Kanlaob River in Alegria going through Badian and ending in Kawasan Falls.

The second one is the upstream canyoneering that simply reverses the downstream route. The starting point of the upstream canyoneering is in the Ka You sent Kawasan Falls, going through Badian and all the way to Kanlaob River.
