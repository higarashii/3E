White Island

Nestled off the coast of Camiguin, a captivating island province in the Philippines, lies the breathtaking White Island. This pristine sandbar is a picturesque haven, renowned for its unparalleled views of the majestic Mt. Hibok-Hibok volcano and the azure waters that embrace it. Visitors are welcomed to a serene and idyllic tropical paradise, where the immaculate white sand stretches as far as the eye can see, inviting moments of relaxation and wonder amidst the gentle lull of the waves.

White Island beckons travelers seeking respite from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, offering a sanctuary of tranquility and natural beauty. With its crystal-clear waters and soft, powdery sand, it presents an ideal setting for leisurely strolls, sunbathing, or simply basking in the splendor of nature's bounty. Whether one seeks adventure or solace, this enchanting destination promises an unforgettable escape, where time seems to stand still amidst the harmonious symphony of land, sea, and sky.

As the sun dips below the horizon, casting hues of gold and pink across the sky, White Island transforms into a mesmerizing spectacle, painting an ethereal backdrop for romantic moments or quiet reflection. Whether marveling at the grandeur of Mt. Hibok-Hibok, diving into the crystalline waters teeming with vibrant marine life, or simply reveling in the serenity of this pristine oasis, White Island captivates the hearts and imaginations of all who are fortunate enough to experience its allure.

How to get there

To reach White Island, you can follow these general directions:

Travelers typically embark on a short boat journey from Yumbing or Agoho Beach in Mambajao, the capital of Camiguin, to reach White Island. These boat trips are conveniently available and usually take between 15 to 20 minutes, offering a smooth and swift passage to the idyllic sandbar.

Tourists have the option to organize either a private boat rental for a more personalized experience or join a group excursion arranged by local tour companies or accommodations, ensuring hassle-free access to this enchanting destination.
