GLORIA'S FANTASYLAND

Waterfront theme park with many rides & live entertainment plus shopping, parades & fireworks. The park is located in Dapitan City, Zamboanga del Norte. Glorious Fantasyland opened its doors in 2009 as the brainchild of Zamboanga del Norte's Jalosjos family who also owns Dakak. What started out as a humble pint-sized perya (or fair) has since expanded to a five-hectare theme park with rides, attractions, and shows to keep visitors entertained. How to get there

To reach Gloria’s Fantasyland, you can follow these general directions:

By Public Transportation:

1. If you're using public transportation, your first step is to travel to Zamboanga City. You can take a bus, van, or plane depending on your location. From Zamboanga City, head to a tricycle or jeepney terminal. Inform the driver that you're going to Gloria's Fantasyland in Santa Maria. They should be familiar with the destination and can drop you off at the nearest point.

2. Once you're dropped off, you may need to walk a short distance to reach Gloria's Fantasyland. Follow signs or ask locals for directions if needed.

By Private Vehicle:

1. Use a map or GPS to plan your route to Gloria's Fantasyland. The theme park is located in Zamboanga City, typically in the vicinity of Santa Maria, which is a barangay in the city. If you're coming from outside Zamboanga City, your first step is to travel to the city. Major highways and roads usually lead to Zamboanga City from neighboring towns and cities.

2. Once you're in Zamboanga City, drive to the barangay of Santa Maria. This is where Gloria's Fantasyland is typically located. Follow signs or ask locals for directions to the theme park. Upon arrival at Gloria's Fantasyland, there should be designated parking areas where you can safely park your vehicle. Be prepared to pay a parking fee if applicable.
