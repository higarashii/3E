St. Matthias Parish Church

St. Matthias Parish Church, located in Tumauini, Isabela, is a historical landmark known for its unique architecture and cultural significance. Built in the 18th century, this centuries-old church features a distinct cylindrical bell tower, making it one of the only two round churches in the Philippines. The church's design reflects a blend of Spanish colonial and native Filipino influences, showcasing intricate details and craftsmanship.

Visitors to St. Matthias Parish Church can admire its well-preserved facade adorned with ornate carvings and embellishments. Inside, the church boasts a serene atmosphere with its high ceilings, wooden beams, and religious artwork. The church also houses antique wooden altars and religious relics, adding to its historical charm.

Apart from its architectural beauty, St. Matthias Parish Church holds cultural significance as a symbol of faith and heritage for the local community. It serves as a venue for religious ceremonies, gatherings, and celebrations, attracting pilgrims and tourists alike to experience its spiritual ambiance and rich history.

How to get there

To get to St. Matthias Parish Church in Tumauini, Isabela, you can follow these general directions:

1. By Land: If you're coming from Manila or other parts of Luzon, you can take a bus bound for Tuguegarao City, which is the capital of Cagayan Province. From Tuguegarao, you can then take another bus or a van going to Tumauini, Isabela. Alternatively, you can drive to Tumauini if you have your own vehicle or rent one.

2. By Air: The nearest airport with commercial flights is Tuguegarao Airport (TUG), which serves Tuguegarao City. From there, you can take a bus, van, or arrange for a private vehicle to reach Tumauini.

Once you arrive in Tumauini, you can ask locals for directions to St. Matthias Parish Church, as it is a prominent landmark in the town and easily accessible from the main roads.
